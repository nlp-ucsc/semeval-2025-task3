Richard Vaughn Spencer has held several prominent positions throughout his career in business, government, and the military:

1. United States Secretary of the Navy: Spencer served as the 76th Secretary of the Navy from August 3, 2017 to November 24, 2019[1][2].

2. Acting United States Secretary of Defense: He briefly held this position from July 15 to July 23, 2019[1][2].

3. Acting United States Deputy Secretary of Defense: Spencer served in this role from July 24 to July 31, 2019[1][2].

4. U.S. Marine Corps: He served as a Marine Aviator from 1976 to 1981, reaching the rank of captain[1].

5. Vice Chairman and Chief Financial Officer of Intercontinental Exchange (IEX): Spencer held this position from November 2001 to January 2008[1][4].

6. President of Lehman Crossroads Investment Advisers LP: He served in this role from 1998 to 2001[4].

7. Senior Vice President-Private Funds Group at Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp[4].

8. President of Cross Roads Investment Management[4].

9. Chairman at Bondi Partners LLC: This is one of his current positions[4].

10. Chairman at Austal Ltd: As of July 2024, Spencer replaced John Rothwell as chairman of this company[1][4].

11. Director positions: He currently serves as a director for several companies, including Aviation Safety Resources, Inc., Morpheus Space, Inc., Global Atlantic Financial Group LLC, AxonPrime Infrastructure Acquisition Corp., OneWeb Technologies, Inc., Focused Energy, Inc., and Prime Movers Lab LLC[4].

12. Defense Business Board: Spencer served on this Pentagon advisory panel from 2009 to 2015[1][2].

13. Chief of Naval Operations Executive Panel: He was a member of this panel[1][2].

Throughout his career, Spencer has also worked in financial services for various firms including Goldman Sachs, Bear Stearns, A. G. Becker, Paine Webber, and Merrill Lynch[1][2].
