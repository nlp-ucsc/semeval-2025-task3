La Chapelle-Thouarault is a commune located in the Ille-et-Vilaine department in the Brittany region of northwestern France[1][2]. It is situated about 10 kilometers west of the city of Rennes[2]. The commune is part of Rennes Métropole, the intercommunal structure centered on Rennes[1][5].

Some key details about La Chapelle-Thouarault:

- It has an area of 7.64 square kilometers[4]
- The population was 2,252 as of 2021[2][4]
- Its elevation ranges from 28 to 48 meters above sea level[4]
- The postal code is 35590[4]

La Chapelle-Thouarault is located in a rural area west of Rennes, bordering several other small communes. Its proximity to Rennes has allowed it to be incorporated into the Rennes metropolitan area and benefit from the economic development of the larger city while maintaining its own distinct identity as a smaller town[2].
