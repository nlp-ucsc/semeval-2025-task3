Domantas Sabonis has played for three NBA teams during his professional career:

1. Oklahoma City Thunder (2016-2017)
Sabonis was drafted 11th overall by the Orlando Magic in the 2016 NBA draft but was immediately traded to the Oklahoma City Thunder on draft night[1][3]. He spent his rookie season with the Thunder, playing 81 games and starting in 66 of them[1].

2. Indiana Pacers (2017-2022)
On July 6, 2017, Sabonis was traded to the Indiana Pacers along with Victor Oladipo in exchange for Paul George[3]. He played for the Pacers for five seasons, from 2017 to 2022. During his time with the Pacers, Sabonis developed into an All-Star player, earning two All-Star selections[1][2].

3. Sacramento Kings (2022-present)
In February 2022, Sabonis was traded to the Sacramento Kings[2][3]. He has been playing for the Kings since then and is currently in his third season with the team. With the Kings, Sabonis has continued to excel, earning another All-Star selection and being named to the All-NBA Third Team twice (2022-23 and 2023-24 seasons)[2].
