The main actors in the movie "Léon: The Professional" are:

1. Jean Reno as Léon Montana, the titular professional hitman[1][3][4].

2. Natalie Portman as Mathilda Lando, the 12-year-old girl who becomes Léon's protégée. This was Portman's feature film debut[1][3][4].

3. Gary Oldman as Norman Stansfield, the corrupt and psychotic DEA agent who serves as the film's main antagonist[1][3][4].

4. Danny Aiello as Tony, the mafioso who gives Léon his assignments[1][3][4].

Supporting cast members include:

5. Peter Appel as Malky, one of Stansfield's corrupt DEA agents[1][3].

6. Michael Badalucco as Mathilda's father[1].

7. Ellen Greene as Margie Lando, Mathilda's stepmother[1].

8. Elizabeth Regen as Mathilda's sister[4].

9. Carl J. Matusovich as Mathilda's younger brother[4].

The film was directed by Luc Besson, who also wrote the screenplay[1][3]. It was released in 1994 and has since become a cult classic, particularly noted for the performances of Reno, Portman (in her breakout role), and Oldman[7].
