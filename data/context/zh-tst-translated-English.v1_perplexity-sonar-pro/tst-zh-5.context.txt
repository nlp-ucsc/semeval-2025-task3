Mario Bolatti's professional football career began at Club Atlético Belgrano in 2003[1][2]. Belgrano is a club based in Córdoba, Argentina. Bolatti started playing for Belgrano when they were in the Primera B Nacional, which is the second tier of Argentine football[7]. He remained with Belgrano until 2007, helping the club gain promotion to the Argentine Primera División in 2006[1][2]. During his time at Belgrano, Bolatti established himself as a promising young midfielder before moving on to other clubs later in his career.
