Ludhiana, located in the Punjab region of India, has a significant Sikh population. The city is one of the largest in Punjab and serves as a major cultural and economic hub for the Sikh community. Many Sikhs reside in Ludhiana, contributing to the city's diverse and vibrant culture. Gurdwaras, or Sikh temples, are prominent in the city, reflecting the strong presence of Sikhism in the area. Ludhiana's economy thrives with numerous industries, and the Sikh community plays an active role in business and trade, enhancing the city's prosperity.

In summary, Ludhiana indeed has a substantial Sikh presence, shaping both the cultural landscape and economic activities of the city.
