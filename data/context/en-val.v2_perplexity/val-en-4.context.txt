Chance the Rapper debuted in the music scene with his first mixtape, *10 Day*, which was released on April 3, 2012[1][2][4]. This mixtape marked his initial entry into the hip-hop world and gained him significant attention from both critics and audiences. His subsequent mixtapes, including *Acid Rap* and *Coloring Book*, further solidified his position in the music industry and led to widespread acclaim and multiple Grammy Awards[1][2][4].
