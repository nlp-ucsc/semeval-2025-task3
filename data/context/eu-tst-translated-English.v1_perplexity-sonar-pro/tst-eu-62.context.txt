Kayle Browning was born in Conway, Arkansas[1][2]. Conway is located in Faulkner County, Arkansas. Browning was born on July 9, 1992, to parents Tommy Lynn Browning and Tammy Browning[1]. She grew up in the area, attending Greenbrier High School in nearby Greenbrier, Arkansas, from which she graduated in 2010[1]. After high school, Browning went on to study interior design at the University of Central Arkansas (UCA), which is also located in Conway[1].
