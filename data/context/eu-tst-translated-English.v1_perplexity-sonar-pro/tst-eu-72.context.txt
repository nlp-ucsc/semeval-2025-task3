Sophie Nélisse won several awards for her performance in "The Book Thief":

1. Satellite Award for Breakthrough Performance[1][3][4]

2. Hollywood Film Festival Spotlight Award[1][3][4]

3. Phoenix Film Critics Society Award for Best Performance by a Youth in a Leading or Supporting Role - Female[1][2][3][4]

4. Young Artist Award for Best Leading Young Actress in a Feature Film[1][3][4]

5. Nevada Film Critics Society Award for Best Youth Performance[3]

In total, Sophie Nélisse won 5 awards specifically for her role in "The Book Thief". Additionally, she received nominations for other awards, including a Critics' Choice Movie Award nomination for Best Young Actor/Actress[1][2][3] and a Saturn Award nomination for Best Performance by a Younger Actor[3].
