Netflix released the TV series "The Punisher"[1][4][5]. The series was created for Netflix as part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) television series. The first season was released in its entirety on Netflix on November 17, 2017[1][3][4]. The second and final season was released on January 18, 2019[2].

Some additional details about the series:

1. It was produced by Marvel Television in association with ABC Studios and Bohemian Risk Productions[1].

2. Steve Lightfoot served as the showrunner for both seasons[1][2].

3. The series starred Jon Bernthal as Frank Castle / The Punisher[1].

4. Netflix cancelled "The Punisher" on February 18, 2019, after two seasons[1].

5. All Marvel Netflix series, including "The Punisher", were removed from Netflix on March 1, 2022, after Disney regained the license for them. They began streaming on Disney+ from March 16, 2022[1].
