Solar is the lead vocalist and leader of the South Korean girl group Mamamoo[1][3][7]. Her full name is Kim Yong Sun, and she was born on February 21, 1991[1][7]. Solar debuted with Mamamoo in 2014 and is known for her strong vocal abilities[3]. She is considered the "mother" of the group and also has a successful solo career, including her own YouTube channel called "solarsido"[5][7]. Solar's position in Mamamoo includes main vocalist, leader, and visual[7][9].
