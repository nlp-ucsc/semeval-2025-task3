Based on the search results, the Spanish edition of Dan Brown's novel "Inferno" was published and distributed by PRH Grupo Editorial[1]. Specifically:

1. The Spanish-language paperback edition was published by PRH Grupo Editorial on May 6, 2014[1].

2. The book is part of the "Una novela de Robert Langdon" series and is the 4th book in that series[1].

3. The Spanish edition has 576 pages[1].

PRH Grupo Editorial is likely an abbreviation for Penguin Random House Grupo Editorial, which is the Spanish-language division of Penguin Random House publishing company. This aligns with Dan Brown's other works being published by major imprints of Penguin Random House in English and other languages.
