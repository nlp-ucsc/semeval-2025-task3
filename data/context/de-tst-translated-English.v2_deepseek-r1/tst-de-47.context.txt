According to the 2010 United States Census, Gogebic County, Michigan, had a population of 16,427 people spread across a land area of approximately 3,238 square kilometers. To find the population density, you divide the population by the land area:

16,427 inhabitants ÷ 3,238 km² ≈ 5.07 inhabitants per square kilometer.

So, Gogebic County had about 5.07 people per square kilometer in 2010.
