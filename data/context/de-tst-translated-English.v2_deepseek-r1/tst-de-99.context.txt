Abrus shrubs belong to the **Fabaceae** family, commonly known as the legume or pea family. This family includes a wide variety of plants, many of which are known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities and their characteristic fruit, the legume. Abrus species, such as Abrus precatorius (commonly known as crab's eye or rosary pea), are part of this family and are distributed in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.
