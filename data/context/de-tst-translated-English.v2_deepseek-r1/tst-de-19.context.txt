Chassagnes is a commune in the Hautes-Alpes department in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region of southeastern France. The neighboring municipalities of Chassagnes are:

1. **L'Argentière-la-Bessée** - This town is known for its historical and industrial heritage, particularly in the metallurgical industry, and is located to the north of Chassagnes.
   
2. **Freissinières** - This commune is situated to the east of Chassagnes. It is known for its natural beauty, as it is located in the Vallouise valley.

3. **Saint-André-d'Embrunnais** - Located to the west of Chassagnes, this town is known for its agriculture and rural landscapes.

These municipalities border Chassagnes and contribute to the region's diverse culture and geography.
