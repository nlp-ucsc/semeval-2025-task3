Oxford University Association Football Club, commonly known as Oxford University AFC, competes in the British Universities & Colleges Sport (BUCS) football leagues. Specifically, they are part of BUCS's top-tier league, which is the BUCS Premier League. The club also participates in cup competitions organized by BUCS. Additionally, Oxford University AFC has a long-standing rivalry with Cambridge University AFC, with whom they contest the annual Varsity Match. The club has also competed in the FA Amateur Cup in the past, although their participation in external cup competitions can vary based on their performance and scheduling.
