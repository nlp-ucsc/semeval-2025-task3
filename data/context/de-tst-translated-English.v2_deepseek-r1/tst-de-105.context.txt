The American band HEALTH has released several studio albums. Their discography includes:

1. **HEALTH** - Their self-titled debut album, released in 2007.
2. **Get Color** - Their second album, released in 2009.
3. **DEATH MAGIC** - Their third album, released in 2015.
4. **VOL 4 :: SLAVES OF FEAR** - Their fourth album, released in 2019.

Each of these albums showcases the band's experimental sound, blending noise rock, electronic, and industrial elements.
