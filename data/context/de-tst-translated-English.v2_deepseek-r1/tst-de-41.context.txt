Adolf Galland, the German fighter pilot during World War II, had three brothers, all of whom were also involved in military service. His brothers were Wilhelm-Ferdinand "Wutz" Galland, Paul Galland, and Kurt Galland. All three brothers served in the German Luftwaffe during the war. Adolf Galland is one of the most famous aces of the war, known for his exceptional flying skills and his role as a leader in the Luftwaffe.
