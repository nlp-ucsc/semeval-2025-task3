Chuseok is celebrated on the 15th day of the 8th lunar month. In the Gregorian calendar, it typically falls in September or October. For specific dates:
- In 2023, Chuseok is from September 28 to October 1.
- In 2024, it is from October 17 to 21.

It's a significant harvest festival in Korea, emphasizing family reunions, ancestor worship, and traditional activities.
