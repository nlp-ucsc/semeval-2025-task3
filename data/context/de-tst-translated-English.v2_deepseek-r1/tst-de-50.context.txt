In Greek mythology, the Aloadae (Ἀλωάδαι) were a pair of gigantic brothers known for their extraordinary strength. Their names were **Otus** (Ὄτυς) and **Ephialtes** (Ἐφιάλτης). They were the sons of Poseidon and were famous for their attempt to storm Mount Olympus by piling mountains upon each other to reach the gods. This story highlights their immense power and audacity.
