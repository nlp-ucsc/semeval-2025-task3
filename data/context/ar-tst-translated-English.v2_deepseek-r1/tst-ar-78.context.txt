"Dissidia Final Fantasy" was released on the PlayStation Portable (PSP) handheld console in 2008. A sequel titled "Dissidia 012 Final Fantasy" was released in 2011, also for the PSP. A follow-up game called "Dissidia Final Fantasy NT" was released for PlayStation 4 in 2018, and it also had a PC version through Steam in 2019. The series primarily focuses on crossover combat between characters from various Final Fantasy titles.
