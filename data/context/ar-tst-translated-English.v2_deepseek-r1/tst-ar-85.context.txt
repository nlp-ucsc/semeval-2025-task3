Aloys Fleischmann is known for his work in the field of musicology. He served as a professor of music at University College Cork (UCC) and was particularly recognized for his expertise in the study and preservation of Irish traditional music. Fleischmann's contributions include the development of the Department of Music at UCC and his role in establishing the Irish Music Archive, now known as the Aloys Fleischmann Room, which houses an extensive collection of materials related to Irish music. His work has been instrumental in promoting and understanding the rich musical heritage of Ireland.
