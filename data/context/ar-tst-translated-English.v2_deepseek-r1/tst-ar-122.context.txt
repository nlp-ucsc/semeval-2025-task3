In the Air France Flight 296 accident, which occurred on June 26, 1988, there were a total of 150 people on board. Unfortunately, 3 passengers died in this incident. The flight was an Airbus A320-100 and crash-landed into a forest near Mulhouse, France. It was a demonstration flight for the aircraft, and despite the tragic loss of life, many survived the accident. This event remains significant in aviation history as it led to important safety improvements.
