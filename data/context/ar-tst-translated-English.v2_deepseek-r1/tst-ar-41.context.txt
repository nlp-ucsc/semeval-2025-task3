The governorate of Tataouine, located in southern Tunisia, is divided into five delegations. These delegations are:

1. **Delegation of Tataouine Nord (Tataouine North)**
2. **Delegation of Tataouine Sud (Tataouine South)**
3. **Delegation of Djerba Ajim**
4. **Delegation of Djerba Mellita**
5. **Delegation of Zarzis**

Each delegation serves as a local administrative unit within the governorate and is responsible for various local governance and administrative tasks.
