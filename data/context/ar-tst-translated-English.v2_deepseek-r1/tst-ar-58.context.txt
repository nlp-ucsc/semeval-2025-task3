The Dash 8 aircraft is currently manufactured by De Havilland Canada, which is a subsidiary of Longview Aviation Capital. De Havilland Canada is responsible for producing the Dash 8-400 aircraft, which is the latest version of the Dash 8 series. Previously, the production of the Dash 8 was handled by Bombardier Aerospace, but in 2019, Longview Aviation Capital acquired the Dash 8 program from Bombardier, and De Havilland Canada was re-established to oversee the manufacturing and support of the aircraft. The Dash 8-400 is a popular regional turboprop aircraft known for its reliability, comfort, and efficiency, and it is used by airlines around the world for short to medium-haul routes.
