The town of Fawara is located in the Lecco province of Lombardy, Italy. The official language spoken in Fawara, as in the rest of Italy, is Italian. However, it's worth noting that Lombardy has its own regional language, Lombard, which is recognized and protected. In addition, since Fawara is part of the Italian-speaking community, Italian remains the predominant and official language used in government, education, and daily life.
