Chance the Rapper made his debut in April 2012 with the release of his first mixtape titled "10 Day." He created this mixtape during a ten-day suspension from high school, which is how it got its name. "10 Day" garnered attention in the hip-hop community and set the stage for his subsequent success. His follow-up mixtape, "Acid Rap," released in 2013, further boosted his popularity and critical acclaim.
