No, Chris Claremont was not the sole writer of the "Days of Future Past" storyline. It was co-written by Chris Claremont and John Byrne. The story was published in "The Uncanny X-Men" issues #141 and #142 in 1981, with John Byrne also serving as the artist. The collaboration between Claremont and Byrne was significant in shaping this influential storyline in the X-Men series.
