Folorunso Alakija's foundation is indeed called the *Rose of Sharon Foundation*. This foundation focuses on empowering widows and orphans through various initiatives, including education, healthcare, and financial support. It aims to improve the quality of life for these vulnerable groups in Nigeria. The foundation is a significant part of Folorunsho Alakija's philanthropic efforts, reflecting her commitment to giving back to the community and making a positive impact on society.
