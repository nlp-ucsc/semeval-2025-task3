The Leftovers aired for 3 seasons[1][2]. The series premiered on HBO on June 29, 2014 and concluded on June 4, 2017[1]. Over the course of its run, a total of 28 episodes were produced across the three seasons[1][2]:

- Season 1 consisted of 10 episodes and aired from June 29 to September 7, 2014[2].
- Season 2 also had 10 episodes and aired from October 4 to December 6, 2015[2].
- The third and final season was slightly shorter with 8 episodes, airing from April 16 to June 4, 2017[1][2].

The show was created by Damon Lindelof and Tom Perrotta, based on Perrotta's novel of the same name. It starred an ensemble cast including Justin Theroux, Amy Brenneman, Christopher Eccleston, Liv Tyler, and Carrie Coon[1]. While the first season covered the entirety of the source novel, the second and third seasons featured original material expanding beyond the book[1].
