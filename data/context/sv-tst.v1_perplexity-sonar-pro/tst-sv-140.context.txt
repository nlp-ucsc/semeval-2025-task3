Dan Kimball was the pastor of Vintage Faith Church in Santa Cruz, California[1][3][7]. Specifically:

- Kimball planted and started Vintage Faith Church in 2004[1].
- He served as the lead pastor of Vintage Faith Church[1][3].
- Vintage Faith Church is known for operating The Abbey coffeehouse on Mission Street in Santa Cruz[3].
- The church's vision statement identifies their desire to be a "worshipping community of missional theologians"[9].
- Vintage Faith Church focuses on rethinking church through a missional lens for new generations and culture, including using creative forms of worship like art and prayer stations[9].

Prior to starting Vintage Faith Church, Kimball served on staff at Santa Cruz Bible Church for 13 years[7]. He left that position to plant Vintage Faith as a new church in 2004.
