Le Mesnil-Opac is a municipality in the Normandy region of France. The climate in this region is generally temperate maritime, characterized by mild winters and cool summers. It is influenced by the Gulf Stream, which brings warmth from the Atlantic Ocean. 

In Le Mesnil-Opac, you can expect:

- **Winters** to be mild with temperatures rarely dropping below freezing.
- **Summers** to be warm but not excessively hot, with average temperatures around 20-25°C (68-77°F).
- **Precipitation** is fairly evenly distributed throughout the year, with some rain in most months.
- **Autumn and spring** are usually mild and pleasant, with colorful foliage in autumn.

Overall, the climate is quite pleasant, making it suitable for agriculture and outdoor activities.
