The podium for the Taekwondo event at the 2012 Summer Olympics - Women's over 67 kg was as follows:

1. **Gold Medal**: Anne-Caroline Graffe (France)
2. **Silver Medal**: Anika Krebs (Morocco)
3. **Bronze Medal**: Anastasia Baryshnikova (Russia)

This event took place at ExCeL London in London, United Kingdom, on August 11, 2012.
