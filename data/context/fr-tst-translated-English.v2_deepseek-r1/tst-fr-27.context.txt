The genus *Foudia* includes seven species of birds commonly known as fodies. These are:

1. **Red Fody (*Foudia madagascariensis*)**
2. **Forest Fody (*Foudia omissa*)**
3. **Mauritius Fody (*Foudia rubra*)**
4. **Seychelles Fody (*Foudia sechellarum*)**
5. ** Rodrigues Fody (*Foudia flavicans*)**
6. **Comoros Fody (*Foudia bromacha*)**
7. **Chestnut-winged Fody (*Foudia vinacea*)**

These species are primarily found in Madagascar and the Mascarene Islands.
