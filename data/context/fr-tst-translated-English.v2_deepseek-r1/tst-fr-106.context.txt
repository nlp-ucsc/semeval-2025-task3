Sophie Nélisse won the Genie Award for Best Supporting Actress for her role as Gabrielle Dube in the film *Gabrielle*. The film was released in 2013, and she received the award at the 2nd Canadian Screen Awards in 2014.

The Genie Awards, now known as the Canadian Screen Awards, recognize excellence in Canadian cinema. Sophie Nélisse’s performance in *Gabrielle* was highly acclaimed and earned her this prestigious award.
