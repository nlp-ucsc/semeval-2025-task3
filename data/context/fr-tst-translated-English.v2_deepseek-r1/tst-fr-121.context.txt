The 1931 Ice Hockey World Championships were held in Krynica, Poland, from February 1 to February 8, 1931. The tournament involved five teams: Canada, the United States, Austria, Germany, and Poland.

In the first round, the teams were paired as follows:

1. **Canada vs. Poland**: Canada won the match with a score of 9-0.
2. **Austria vs. United States**: The United States won the match with a score of 2-1.

Germany received a bye in the first round and advanced directly to the next stage.

These results set the stage for the subsequent matches in the tournament, ultimately leading to Canada being crowned the champion.
