The village of Pferdingsleben is first mentioned under the name "Pferideslebo" in a document from the year 780. This document is part of the historical records of the Hersfeld Abbey ( Reichsbessitz des Klosters Hersfeld), which was an important monastery in the region during the Carolingian era.

So, the first recorded mention of Pferdingsleben is as "Pferideslebo" in the year 780.
