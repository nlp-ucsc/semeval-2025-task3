According to the *Trésor de la Langue Française Informatisé* (TLFi), which is the digitized version of the *Tresor de la Langue Française*, an empire is defined as a vast political entity characterized by a central power that has authority over multiple various peoples and territories. The term is often used to describe forms of governance where there is a dominant state or sovereign who has control over other states, provinces, or colonies. The empire can be both a political structure and a historical concept, referring to various periods and forms of domination throughout history.

In summary, the TLFi defines "empire" as a large and powerful state or political entity that exerts control over several territories and peoples, often through a central authority.
