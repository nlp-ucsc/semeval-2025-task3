The Punisher series was originally shown on Netflix. It premiered on Netflix on November 17, 2017 and ran for two seasons, with the second season releasing on January 18, 2019[1][4].

However, Netflix cancelled the series on February 18, 2019. On March 1, 2022, all of the Marvel Netflix series, including The Punisher, were removed from Netflix after Disney regained the license for them[1].

Currently, The Punisher is available to stream on Disney+. It was added to Disney+ on March 16, 2022, along with the other former Netflix Marvel series[1][3]. 

Viewers can now watch both seasons of The Punisher on Disney+[2][3]. Additionally, the series is available for purchase on some digital platforms like Apple TV, Fandango At Home, and Microsoft Store[3].
