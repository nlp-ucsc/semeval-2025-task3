Edu Manga played for the Brazil national team[1][2][3]. He represented Brazil in international matches between 1987 and 1989, earning a total of 10 caps for the national team[2][3]. Specifically:

- In 1987, he made 2 appearances for Brazil
- In 1988, he made 3 appearances 
- In 1989, he made 5 appearances

Edu Manga was also part of Brazil's squad for the 1987 Copa América tournament[2]. However, he did not score any goals in his 10 appearances for the national team[2][3].

It's worth noting that Edu Manga's full name is Eduardo Antônio dos Santos, and he was born on February 2, 1967, in Osasco, São Paulo, Brazil[1][2]. He primarily played as a midfielder during his professional career[2].
