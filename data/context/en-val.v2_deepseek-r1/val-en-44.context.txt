The Sakya Trizin is the spiritual leader of the Sakyapa school, one of the four main traditions of Tibetan Buddhism. The Sakya Trizin is both the spiritual and temporal head of the Sakya lineage, holding significant religious authority and caring for the welfare of the Sakya community.
