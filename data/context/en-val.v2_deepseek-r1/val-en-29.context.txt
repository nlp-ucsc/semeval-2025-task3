The largest ethnic group in Katsina State, Nigeria is the Hausa people. The Hausa are the dominant ethnic group in the state and have a significant influence on the culture, language, and political landscape of Katsina. They are known for their rich cultural heritage, including their language, clothing, and traditional practices. Additionally, Katsina State is also home to other ethnic groups, but the Hausa remain the most populous and influential group in the region.
