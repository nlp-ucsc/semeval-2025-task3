The headquarters of the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD) is located in Geneva, Switzerland. UNCTAD is a part of the United Nations Secretariat and operates under the United Nations General Assembly. It is one of the key United Nations agencies focused on promoting development and cooperation in international trade, with an emphasis on addressing the needs of developing countries. The headquarters in Geneva serves as the central hub for its activities and hosts various conferences and meetings related to global trade and development issues.
