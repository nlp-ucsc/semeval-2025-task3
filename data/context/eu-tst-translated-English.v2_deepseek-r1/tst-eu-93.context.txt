The Kaiser Chiefs are from Leeds, West Yorkshire, in England, UK. Formed in 2000, the band consists of Ricky Wilson (lead vocals), Andrew "Whitey" White (guitar), Simon Rix (bass), Nick "Peanut" Baines (keyboards), and Vijay Mistry (drums, since 2016). They are known for their indie rock and new wave music style and have had several hit songs like "I Predict a Riot" and "Ruby."
