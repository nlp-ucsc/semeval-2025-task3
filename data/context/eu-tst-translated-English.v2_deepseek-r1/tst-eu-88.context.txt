The Leftovers, an American television drama series created by Damon Lindelof and Tom Perrotta, consists of three seasons. The show premiered on June 29, 2014, and the final episode aired on June 4, 2017. Each season explores themes of grief, loss, and existential crisis following a global event in which 2% of the world's population vanishes without explanation. The series is known for its complex characters, emotional depth, and philosophical themes.
