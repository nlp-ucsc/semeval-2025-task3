The Royal Yacht **Britannia** is indeed still in existence and is now a popular museum and event venue in Edinburgh, Scotland. Although it was decommissioned from the British Royal Navy in 1997, the ship was preserved and is now permanently moored in the port of Leith. Visitors can explore various sections of the yacht, including the State Rooms, the Engine Room, and even the Queen's private quarters. Britannia has become a significant historical and cultural landmark, offering insights into the history of the British Royal Family and their travels. 

Therefore, the HMY Britannia can be found and visited today in Leith, Scotland.
