Catherine McCormack is an English actress known for her work in film, television, and theatre. She has appeared in a variety of roles, showcasing her versatility as a performer.

Some of her notable works include:

1. **Braveheart** (1995) - She played the role of Murron MacClannough.
2. **The Land Girls** (1998) - She portrayed Stella.
3. **Dancing at Lughnasa** (1998) - She played the role of Agnes Mundy.
4. **Shadowlands** (1993) - She appeared as Laura Ponsonby.
5. **The Weight of Water** (2000) - She played the role of Sarah Pease.

Catherine McCormack is recognized for her depth and nuance in her performances, contributing to various compelling stories across different mediums.
