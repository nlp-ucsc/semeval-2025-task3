Pietro Nenni was an Italian political figure whose ideology evolved significantly over his lifetime. Initially, he was associated with the **Italian Socialist Party (PSI)** and held **socialist and anti-fascist** views. During the post-World War II era, he played a key role in the unity of the political left. Nenni was instrumental in the formation of the **Popular Democratic Front (FDP)**, which aimed to unify socialist and communist forces. His political stance was aligned with **democratic socialism**, and he later became a prominent figure in the **Italian Socialist Party (PSI)**, advocating for progressive reforms and social justice. Nenni's ideology was rooted in promoting democracy, workers' rights, and anti-fascism throughout his career.
