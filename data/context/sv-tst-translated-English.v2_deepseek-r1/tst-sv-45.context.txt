Liu Wenhui was born in 1895 in Anjie, Sichuan, China. He received a classical education in his early years, studying the Confucian classics and traditional Chinese literature. Later, he attended theItemClickListener military academy in Chengdu, Sichuan, where he received military training and education. This academy was known for producing many military officers and leaders during the late Qing and early Republican periods in China. Liu Wenhui's education and training at the academy laid the foundation for his future career as a prominent warlord in Sichuan Province during the Warlord Era of the 1920s.
