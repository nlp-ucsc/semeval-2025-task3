Laurance Rockefeller was the chairman of the Rockefeller Brothers Fund between 1958 and 1968. He later served as the president of the Rockefeller Family & Associates and was involved with numerous other Rockefeller-associated organizations, but the specific position of chairman in the timeframe between 1968 and 1980 that you're asking about would be at the Rockefeller Brothers Fund until 1968. Beyond that, he continued to hold significant roles in the family's philanthropic efforts.
