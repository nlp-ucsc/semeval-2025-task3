The city of Ożarów, located in the Sandomierz County of the Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship in southeastern Poland, has a history that dates back to the medieval period. It was founded in the 12th century, with the first written records of the settlement appearing in the 13th century. Ożarów received its town rights around 1569, making it a significant urban center in the region during the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth. Over the centuries, it has experienced various phases of development and decline, influenced by historical events such as wars and changes in political borders. Today, Ożarów is a charming town with a rich history and cultural heritage.
