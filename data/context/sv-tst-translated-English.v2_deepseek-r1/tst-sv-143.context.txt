The asteroid named after Sergei Parajanov is **4515 Khrennikov**, not directly after Parajanov. However, there is an asteroid named **2850 Jasu-Juku** which is named in honor of a character from Parajanov's film "The Color of Pomegranates." Additionally, **4523josmar** does not correspond to any known asteroid related to Parajanov.
