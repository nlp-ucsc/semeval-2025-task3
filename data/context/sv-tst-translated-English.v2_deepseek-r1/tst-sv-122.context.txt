Russ Meyer directed "Faster, Pussycat! Kill! Kill!" in 1965. This film is one of his most famous works and is often regarded as a cult classic. It's known for its over-the-top action, campy dialogue, and strong female characters. Meyer's directing style blended elements of exploitation cinema with dark humor and a distinctive visual aesthetic. "Faster, Pussycat! Kill! Kill!" has become a cult favorite and is celebrated for its bold and irreverent approach to storytelling.
