The Cappella De Sylva in Sant'Isidoro a Capo le Case was designed by the renowned Italian architect Carlo Mariani. Mariani, who was active during the 20th century, is known for his work in the field of liturgical design and architecture. His design for the chapel reflects a blend of modern and traditional elements, creating a space that is both aesthetically pleasing and spiritually evocative. Mariani's attention to detail and his ability to incorporate natural materials contribute to the chapel's harmonious and contemplative atmosphere.
