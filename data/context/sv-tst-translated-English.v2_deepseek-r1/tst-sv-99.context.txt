The movie "Tuck Everlasting" is based on the book of the same name, "Tuck Everlasting," written by Natalie Babbitt. The book was first published in 1975 and has since become a classic of children's literature. The story revolves around a family who discovers a magical spring that grants eternal life and the moral dilemmas that come with such a gift. The book has been adapted into several film versions, including a 2002 Disney adaptation.
