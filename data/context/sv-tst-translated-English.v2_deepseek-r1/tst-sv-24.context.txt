Asteroid (7532) Pelhřimov, discovered by Zdeněk Moravec in 1995, is named after the Czech town of Pelhřimov, which is also known as the "Gateway to the Highlands." The asteroid is part of the inner asteroid belt and has an orbital period of approximately 5.57 years. Zdeněk Moravec is a Czech astronomer who has discovered several asteroids, and his work contributes significantly to our understanding of the solar system.
