Hamilton Academical F.C., a Scottish professional football club, hosts their home games at New Douglas Park, which is often referred to as the Fountain of Youth Stadium. This stadium is situated in Hamilton, South Lanarkshire, Scotland. New Douglas Park has been the home ground for Hamilton Academical since its opening in 2001. With a capacity of over 6,000 spectators, it provides an intimate setting for fans to watch the team in action. The nickname "Fountain of Youth Stadium" is a nod to the club's reputation for developing young talent through their renowned youth academy. This nickname reflects their commitment to nurturing young players, many of whom have gone on to have successful careers in football. The stadium's facilities are well-equipped, offering a good matchday experience for both home and away supporters.
