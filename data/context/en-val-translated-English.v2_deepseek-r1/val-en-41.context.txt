Edu Manga is indeed the stage name of Eduardo Antônio dos Santos, a Brazilian former professional footballer. Born on February 28, 1967, in São Paulo, Brazil, Edu Manga was known for his career as a midfielder and was particularly famous for his time playing for the Brazilian national team. He participated in the 1987 Pan American Games, where Brazil won the gold medal, and also played in the 1988 Seoul Olympics. Edu Manga's career included stints with several clubs, both in Brazil and internationally. His skills on the field made him a notable figure in Brazilian football during his active years.
