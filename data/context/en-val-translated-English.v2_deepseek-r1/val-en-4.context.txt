Chance the Rapper, whose real name is Chancelor Johnathan Bennett, made his debut with his mixtape "10 Day" in 2012. This project was his first major release and marked the beginning of his meteoric rise in the music industry. The mixtape, which was released while Chance was still in high school, showcased his unique blend of hip-hop, gospel, and introspective lyrics, setting the stage for his future success. "10 Day" was well-received by critics and helped establish Chance as a promising new talent in the Chicago hip-hop scene. Some notable tracks from the mixtape include "15," "21," and " GTBS (Got That in A Bag)."
