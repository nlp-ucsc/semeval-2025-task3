Monterchi is located in the Province of Arezzo in the Italian region of Tuscany[1][2]. The town is situated about 80 kilometers (50 miles) southeast of Florence and approximately 20 kilometers (12 miles) east of Arezzo[1]. Monterchi sits in the northern part of Valtiberina (Tiber Valley), where the Tiber river flows from Emilia-Romagna towards Rome[1].
