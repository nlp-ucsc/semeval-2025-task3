According to the 2010 census, there were 681 households in Manteo, North Carolina[3]. This information comes from the detailed census data provided for Manteo in the search results. The 2010 census reported a total population of 1,434 people living in these 681 households, with an average household size of 2.11 persons[3].

To provide some additional context:

- Of these 681 households, 373 (54.8%) were family households, while 308 (45.2%) were non-family households[3].
- 25.4% of households had children under the age of 18 living with them[3].
- 38.8% were married couples living together[3].
- 12.5% had a female householder with no husband present[3].
- 39.1% of all households were made up of individuals living alone[3].
- 14.7% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older[3].
