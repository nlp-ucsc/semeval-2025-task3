Inferno is the 4th book in the Robert Langdon series by Dan Brown[1][2][4][5]. The Robert Langdon books in order are:

1. Angels & Demons (2000)
2. The Da Vinci Code (2003)
3. The Lost Symbol (2009)
4. Inferno (2013)
5. Origin (2017)

Inferno was published in 2013 and follows Robert Langdon as he wakes up in a hospital in Florence, Italy with no memory of how he got there. He then has to escape assassins and decipher clues related to Dante's Inferno while trying to recover his recent memories[5][7]. The book was later adapted into a film starring Tom Hanks as Robert Langdon.
