Yes, there is a member of Kaiser Chiefs who plays the keyboard, which includes piano. Nick Baines, also known by his nickname "Peanut," is the band's keyboardist[1][2][4]. He has been with the band since its formation in 2000 (originally under different names) and continues to be an active member[1][2][5].

Nick Baines plays various keyboard instruments, including piano, synthesizers, and other electronic keyboards, contributing to the band's distinctive sound. He is one of the original members of Kaiser Chiefs and has been part of all their studio albums and live performances[1][2][4].
