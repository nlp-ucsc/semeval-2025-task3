Vaux-en-Amiénois is located in the Hauts-de-France region of northern France[1][3]. More specifically, it is a commune in the Somme department within the Hauts-de-France region[1][3].

Some key details about Vaux-en-Amiénois:

1. It is situated approximately 8 kilometers (5 miles) northwest of Amiens, the capital city of the Somme department[1].

2. The commune has a population of 430 inhabitants as of 2022[4].

3. Vaux-en-Amiénois has a land area of 11.18 square kilometers[6].

4. The postal code for Vaux-en-Amiénois is 80260[4][6].

5. It is part of the Communauté de communes du Territoire Nord Picardie, an intercommunal structure[4].

The Hauts-de-France region, where Vaux-en-Amiénois is located, was formed in 2016 by merging the former regions of Nord-Pas-de-Calais and Picardy. This region is known for its rich history, diverse landscapes, and strategic location near major European cities like Paris, Lille, and Brussels.
