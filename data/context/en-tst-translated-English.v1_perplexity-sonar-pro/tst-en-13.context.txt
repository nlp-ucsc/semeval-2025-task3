The 2011 UEFA Super Cup was contested between Barcelona and Porto[1][4][6]. 

Barcelona qualified for the Super Cup as the winners of the 2010-11 UEFA Champions League, while Porto earned their place by winning the 2010-11 UEFA Europa League[4][6].

The match took place on Friday, August 26, 2011 at the Stade Louis II in Monaco[1][4]. Barcelona emerged victorious, winning the game 2-0[1][2]. The goals were scored by Lionel Messi in the 39th minute and Cesc FÃ bregas in the 87th minute[1]. 

This victory marked Barcelona's fourth UEFA Super Cup title[7], cementing their status as one of the most successful clubs in the competition's history. For Barcelona, it was their record eighth appearance in the Super Cup[7], highlighting their consistent success in European competitions during that era.

Porto, despite their loss, were making their fourth appearance in the Super Cup[2], demonstrating their own impressive record in European football.
