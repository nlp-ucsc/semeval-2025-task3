The current mayor of Mercy-le-Haut is Roland Bourgeois[1]. Mercy-le-Haut is a commune located in the Meurthe-et-Moselle department in northeastern France. Roland Bourgeois has been serving as the mayor of Mercy-le-Haut since the 2020 municipal elections[1].

Mercy-le-Haut is a small commune with historical significance, notably as the birthplace of Albert Lebrun, who served as the President of France from 1932 to 1940[1][2]. The town is situated in a rural area and, like many French communes, is governed by a mayor and a municipal council.
