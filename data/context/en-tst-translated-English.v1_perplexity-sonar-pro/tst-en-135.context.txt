According to the 2010 census, the population of Palm River-Clair Mel, Florida was 21,024[1][2][4][6][9]. This represents a significant increase from the 2000 census population of 17,589[4]. The community experienced substantial growth between 2000 and 2010, with the population increasing by approximately 19.5% during that decade.

It's worth noting that there is some discrepancy in the reported 2010 population figures across different sources. While most sources consistently report 21,024, one source indicates a slightly higher population of 24,379 for 2010[10]. However, the figure of 21,024 appears to be more widely reported and is likely the official census count.

Since 2010, Palm River-Clair Mel has continued to grow. The 2020 census recorded a population of 26,142[2][6][9], showing further significant growth of about 24.3% between 2010 and 2020. This trend indicates that Palm River-Clair Mel has been experiencing steady population growth over the past few decades.
