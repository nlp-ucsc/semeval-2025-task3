Film, o kterém mluvíte, se jmenuje "Postřižiny". Tento snímek je indeed based on Bohumil Hrabal's book of the same name and was directed by Jiří Menzel in 1980. The film tells the story of a brewery and the family of its manager during the First Czechoslovak Republic. It's a notable work in Czech cinema, capturing the spirit of the period and the unique atmosphere of Hrabal's prose.
