Hüffelsheim is located in Rhineland-Palatinate (Rheinland-Pfalz in German), a state in southwestern Germany[1][3]. More specifically:

- It is situated in the Bad Kreuznach district within Rhineland-Palatinate[1][4].

- Hüffelsheim belongs to the Verbandsgemeinde (collective municipality) of Rüdesheim, whose seat is in the nearby municipality of Rüdesheim an der Nahe[1].

- The town lies on a high plateau at an elevation of about 220 meters above sea level, above the River Nahe[1]. 

- It is located near the Naturpark Soonwald-Nahe, a nature park in the region[1].

- The district seat of Bad Kreuznach is only a few kilometers away to the east-northeast of Hüffelsheim[1].

- Geographically, Hüffelsheim's neighboring municipalities include Rüdesheim an der Nahe to the north, Bad Kreuznach to the east, and several other small towns in the surrounding area[1].

The location in Rhineland-Palatinate places Hüffelsheim in the western part of Germany, relatively close to the borders with France and Luxembourg. This region is known for its wine production, beautiful landscapes, and historical sites.
