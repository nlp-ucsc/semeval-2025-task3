The average temperature in Cilleruelo de San Mamés varies throughout the year:

In winter, temperatures are quite cold. The average temperature in January is around 8°C (46°F), with lows potentially dropping to -3°C (28°F)[4].

Summer temperatures are much warmer. In July, the average temperature rises to 23°C (73°F), with highs potentially reaching up to 36°C (96°F)[7].

Spring and autumn see moderate temperatures. For example, in March, the average temperature is about 8°C (46°F)[4].

Over the course of a year, the temperature typically varies from around 0°C (32°F) in winter to about 28°C (82°F) in summer[1].

It's worth noting that daily temperature fluctuations can be significant. For instance, in July, while daytime highs can reach 36°C (96°F), nighttime lows can drop to 9°C (49°F)[7].

The climate in Cilleruelo de San Mamés is characterized by short, warm, dry summers and very cold winters. The warm season typically lasts for about 2.8 months, from mid-June to early September[1].
