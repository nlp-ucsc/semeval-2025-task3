Chance the Rapper, whose real name is Chancelor Johnathan Bennett, has talked about the music he listened to growing up. He has often cited Kirk Franklin as one of his earliest influences. Kirk Franklin is a prominent gospel singer, songwriter, and producer who is known for blending gospel with hip-hop and R&B. His music likely had a significant impact on Chance's early exposure to both gospel and hip-hop genres, which are both integral to Chance's own musical style.
