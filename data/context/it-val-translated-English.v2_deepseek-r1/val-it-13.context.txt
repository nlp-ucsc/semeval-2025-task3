The most famous confectionery factory in Toruń is the **Kopernik** Factory (formerly known as the **Toruńskie Wody Mineralne Kopernik**). It was opened in **1976** and was founded by the **city of Toruń**. This factory is widely recognized for producing a variety of beloved sweets, including the iconic **KopernikPi desk**, which is a classic Polish chocolate bar. The factory's name pays homage to the famous astronomer **Nicolaus Copernicus**, who was born in Toruń, adding a historical and cultural significance to the brand. Over the years, the factory has become a symbol of Toruń and a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
