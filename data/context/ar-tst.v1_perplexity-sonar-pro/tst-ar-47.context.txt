The film "Sweet Home Alabama" was released on September 27, 2002 in the United States[1][4]. Some additional details about the release:

- It had its premiere at the Sidewalk Moving Picture Festival in the United States on September 20, 2002[1].
- The film's official premiere was held on September 23, 2002 in the United States[1].
- It was released in Canada on the same day as its wide US release - September 27, 2002[1].
- The film was distributed by Buena Vista Pictures through their Touchstone Pictures banner[4].
- It had a successful opening weekend, grossing over $35 million and ranking number one at the box office[4].
- The movie went on to gross over $130 million domestically and another $53 million internationally, making it a box office hit given its $30 million budget[4].

So while there were some earlier festival and premiere screenings, the main theatrical release date for "Sweet Home Alabama" was September 27, 2002 in the United States and Canada.
