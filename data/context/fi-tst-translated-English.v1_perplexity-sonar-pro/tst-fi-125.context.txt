Jelena Naimušina (also spelled Yelena Naimushina) was an Olympic champion in gymnastics. Specifically, she won a gold medal with the Soviet team in the women's gymnastics team all-around event at the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moscow[1][10].

Some key details about Naimušina's Olympic achievement:

1. She competed as part of the Soviet Union's gymnastics team.
2. The gold medal was won in the team all-around event.
3. This victory occurred at the 1980 Summer Olympics held in Moscow.

In addition to her Olympic success, Naimušina also achieved other notable results in her gymnastics career:

- She won a silver medal with the Soviet team at the 1979 World Artistic Gymnastics Championships[1].
- Naimušina was a member of the Soviet national gymnastics team from 1979 to 1980[10].

Sadly, Jelena Naimušina passed away on March 14, 2017, at the age of 52[4][7]. Her unexpected death was a loss to the gymnastics community, as she was remembered as one of the bright Soviet gymnastics stars of the early 1980s.
