Herpy-l'Arlésienne is located in the Ardennes department in northern France[3][4]. The Ardennes department is part of the Grand Est region[3].

Some key details about Herpy-l'Arlésienne:

- It is classified as a commune, which is a type of administrative division in France[3].
- The postal code for Herpy-l'Arlésienne is 08360[4][5].
- It is part of the Canton of Château-Porcien and the Arrondissement of Rethel within the Ardennes department[3].
- The commune has a total area of 10.64 square kilometers[4].
- As of recent estimates, the population is around 213 inhabitants[9].
- The mayor of Herpy-l'Arlésienne is Mr. Claude RÉGNIER[5].

The Ardennes department where Herpy-l'Arlésienne is located borders Belgium and is known for its forested, hilly terrain. The prefecture (administrative capital) of the Ardennes department is Charleville-Mézières, which is about 58 km away from Herpy-l'Arlésienne[4].
