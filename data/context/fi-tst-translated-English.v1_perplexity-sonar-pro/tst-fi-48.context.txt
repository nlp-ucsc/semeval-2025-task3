Catherine McCormack died at home in Noxen, Pennsylvania on Thursday, July 14, 2022[2][8]. She passed away at the age of 75, surrounded by her husband Ralph and son Matt[2]. Noxen is where Catherine had resided with her husband for many years after they moved there from Eglin Air Force Base in Florida[2].

It's important to note that this Catherine McCormack is different from the English actress Catherine McCormack born in 1972, who is known for roles in films like Braveheart and Spy Game[1][3]. The Catherine McCormack who passed away in 2022 was born on December 30, 1946, in Noxen, Pennsylvania[2].
