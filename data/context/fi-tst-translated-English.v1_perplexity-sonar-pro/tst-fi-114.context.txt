David Graf played the character of Sergeant Eugene Tackleberry in the Police Academy movie franchise[1][2][5]. Tackleberry was a gun-enthusiast and trigger-happy police officer known for his love of firearms and overzealous approach to law enforcement[2][5].

Graf portrayed Tackleberry in all seven Police Academy films, starting with the original 1984 movie and continuing through the sequels[4][5]. His character was a consistent presence throughout the series, appearing in:

1. Police Academy (1984)
2. Police Academy 2: Their First Assignment (1985)
3. Police Academy 3: Back in Training (1986)
4. Police Academy 4: Citizens on Patrol (1987)
5. Police Academy 5: Assignment Miami Beach (1988)
6. Police Academy 6: City Under Siege (1989)
7. Police Academy: Mission to Moscow (1994)

Tackleberry became one of the most recognizable and beloved characters in the franchise, known for his enthusiasm for weaponry and his catchphrase "This is my gun!"[2]. Graf's portrayal of Tackleberry was so iconic that it became the role he was best known for throughout his career[1][2][5].

In addition to the films, Graf also reprised his role as Tackleberry in a guest appearance on the short-lived Police Academy: The Series[5], further cementing his association with the character.
