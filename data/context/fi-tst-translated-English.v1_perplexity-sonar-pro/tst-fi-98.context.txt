Dave Tippett started his professional NHL career with the Hartford Whalers in the 1983-84 season[1][3]. After playing college hockey at the University of North Dakota, Tippett signed with the Hartford Whalers as an undrafted free agent[2]. He made his NHL debut with the Whalers in the 1983-84 season, playing 17 games and recording 6 points (4 goals, 2 assists)[3]. Tippett went on to play six full seasons with the Whalers from 1984 to 1990, establishing himself as a defensive specialist and consistent player in the NHL[1][2].
