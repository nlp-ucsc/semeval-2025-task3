The Sacramento Kings are owned by Vivek Ranadivé. He is an Indian-American businessman and the chairman of the NBA team. Ranadivé, who made his fortune in the software industry, purchased a majority stake in the Kings in 2013, helping to keep the team in Sacramento after there were rumors of relocation. He has been instrumental in revitalizing the franchise and is known for his efforts to bring a modern approach to the team's operations.
