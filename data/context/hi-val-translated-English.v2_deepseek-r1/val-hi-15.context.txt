Johnny Cash passed away on September 12, 2003, due to complications from diabetes. He was 71 years old at the time of his death. Cash had been dealing with various health issues throughout his life, including diabetes, which progressively worsened. His wife, June Carter Cash, had passed away just a few months earlier, on May 15, 2003. Johnny Cash's death marked the end of an era for country music and left a lasting legacy in the music industry.
