Razia Sultan, also known as Razia Sultana, was a ruler of the Delhi Sultanate in India. She was the daughter of Sultan Iltutmish and ascended to the throne in 1236, becoming the first and only female ruler of the Delhi Sultanate. Razia Sultan was known for her administrative skills and her efforts to promote justice and education during her reign.

Razia Sultan passed away in 1240. Her reign was marked by both challenges and achievements, and she remains a significant figure in Indian history.
