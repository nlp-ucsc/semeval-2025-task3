 Positioned the film "Police Academy" discusses a group of recruits in a police training program. The main group consists of seven individuals:

1. Carey Mahoney (played by Steve Guttenberg)
2. Moses Hightower (played by Bubba Smith)
3. Larvell Jones (played by Michael Winslow)
4. Douglas Fackler (played by Bruce Mahler)
5. Laverne Hooks (played by Marion Ramsey)
6. Eugene Tackleberry (played by David Graf)
7. George Martin (played by Andrew Rubin)

These seven characters form the core group of recruits throughout the series.
