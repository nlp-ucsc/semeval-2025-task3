The legendary Czech actor who received the Thalia Award for lifetime achievement in 1993 was Josef Kemr[7]. Josef Kemr was a highly respected figure in Czech theater and cinema, known for his versatility and the depth he brought to his performances. The Thalia Awards, which have been presented annually by the Czech Actors' Association since 1993, honored Kemr with their lifetime achievement award in the inaugural year of the awards[7]. This recognition highlights Kemr's significant contributions to Czech performing arts throughout his career.
