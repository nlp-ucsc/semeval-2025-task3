According to the search results, Chance the Rapper listened extensively to Michael Jackson during his elementary school years[2][6]. Specifically, the information indicates that:

1. Michael Jackson was the first artist Chance the Rapper was exposed to musically as a child[6].

2. Chance exclusively listened to Michael Jackson on cassettes until the fifth grade[2].

3. Both Chance and his younger brother, rapper Taylor Bennett, cite Michael Jackson as their first exposure to music while growing up[6].

This early exposure to Michael Jackson's music appears to have been influential in Chance's musical development, though it was later artists like Kanye West who sparked his specific interest in hip-hop[2][6].
