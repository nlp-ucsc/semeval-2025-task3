Valrico is located in Hillsborough County, Florida, United States[1][2]. It is a census-designated place (CDP) situated in the east-central part of Hillsborough County[1]. As of the 2020 census, Valrico had a population of 37,895[1][2]. The community is considered a suburb of Tampa, lying approximately 14 miles east of the city[1][3].

Hillsborough County, where Valrico is situated, is one of Florida's most populous counties. The county seat is Tampa, and it's part of the Tampa-St. Petersburg-Clearwater metropolitan area[4]. Hillsborough County was established in 1834 and has a rich history dating back to the early 19th century[8].
