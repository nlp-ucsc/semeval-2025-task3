Ted Lange appeared in 250 episodes of The Love Boat as bartender Isaac Washington[6]. He was a main cast member throughout the show's entire run from 1977 to 1987[7]. Lange's character Isaac was a fan favorite, known for his friendly demeanor and signature pointed hand gesture in the opening credits[2]. 

As one of the core cast members, Lange appeared in all 9 seasons of the regular series, as well as the pilot episodes and specials[7]. His consistent presence as the ship's bartender made him an integral part of the show's ensemble throughout its decade-long run on ABC.

In addition to acting, Lange also contributed behind the scenes, directing several episodes and writing six episodes of The Love Boat during his time on the series[4]. This demonstrates his multifaceted involvement with the show beyond just his on-screen role.
