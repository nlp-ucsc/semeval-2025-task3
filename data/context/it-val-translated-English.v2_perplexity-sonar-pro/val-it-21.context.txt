The postal code for Hüffelsheim, Germany is 55595[1][3][5][6][7]. Hüffelsheim is located in the state of Rheinland-Pfalz (Rhineland-Palatinate) in the district of Bad Kreuznach[1][3]. It belongs to the Verbandsgemeinde of Rüdesheim, which is a collective municipality in Germany[8].

Some additional details about Hüffelsheim:

- It is situated at coordinates 49.8167° N latitude and 7.8° E longitude[1][3].
- The town is part of the Naturpark Soonwald-Nahe and lies on a high plateau about 220 meters above sea level[8].
- Hüffelsheim's total area is 657 hectares, of which 30 hectares are wooded and 20 hectares are planted with vineyards[8].
- The town has a population of approximately 1,400 residents[8].
- Neighboring municipalities include Rüdesheim an der Nahe, Bad Kreuznach, Traisen, Norheim, Niederhausen, Schloßböckelheim, and Weinsheim[8].

It's worth noting that the 55595 postal code is shared by several other communities in the same district, including Hargesheim, Sankt Katharinen, Roxheim, and Traisen, among others[7].
