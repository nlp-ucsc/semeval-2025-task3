Nora Fatehi is a Canadian actress, model, and dancer who has gained significant popularity in India, particularly in the Bollywood film industry. She was born on February 6, 1992, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Her father is of Moroccan descent, and her mother is of Indian (specifically, Punjabi) and Filipino ancestry. This diverse background contributes to her unique cultural identity. Nora Fatehi is multilingual, fluent in English, Hindi, French, and Arabic, which has helped her connect with a wide audience in her career.
