Chris Claremont's run on *Uncanny X-Men* began with issue #94 in May 1975 and ended with issue #279 in May 1991. His work on the title, often in collaboration with artists like Dave Cockrum and John Byrne, is widely regarded as one of the most influential periods for the X-Men franchise. Over the course of 16 years, Claremont introduced many iconic characters and storylines that continue to shape the Marvel Universe. He returned to the series in 2000 and wrote it again until 2006.
