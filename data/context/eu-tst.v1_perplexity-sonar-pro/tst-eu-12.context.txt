The Boston Celtics play in the National Basketball Association (NBA). They are one of the most storied franchises in NBA history and compete in the Eastern Conference of the league[3]. The Celtics play their home games at TD Garden in Boston, Massachusetts[3].

Some key details about the current 2024-25 Boston Celtics team:

- Their record as of January 25, 2025 is 31-14, which puts them 2nd in the NBA Eastern Conference[3].

- Key players on the roster include Jayson Tatum, Jaylen Brown, Kristaps Porzingis, Jrue Holiday, and Derrick White[3].

- The team is coached by Joe Mazzulla[10].

- They are considered strong contenders to potentially repeat as NBA champions in 2025, after winning the title in 2024[7].

The Celtics have a rich history as one of the NBA's most successful franchises, with 17 championships to their name. They continue to be a powerhouse team in the league, built around All-Stars Jayson Tatum and Jaylen Brown[1][3].
