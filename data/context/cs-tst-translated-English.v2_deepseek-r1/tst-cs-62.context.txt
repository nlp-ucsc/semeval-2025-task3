The Czechoslovak film parody of a western where the main hero only drinks Kolaloka's lemonade is titled "Limonádový Joe aneb Konská opera" ( Lemonade Joe or Horse Opera). This film was released in 1964 and is a classic comedy that parodies American westerns with a focus on humor and satirical elements. The main character, Lemonade Joe, is known for only drinking Kolaloka's lemonade, which becomes a central gag in the movie.
