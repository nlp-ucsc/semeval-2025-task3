The founder of the Škoda engineering company was Count Franz von Nepomuk(Level of Count) during the 19th century. He established the company in 1859 in Plzeň, Bohemia, which was then part of the Austrian Empire. Initially, the company focused on producing machinery and equipment, and over time, it grew into a prominent industrial conglomerate, especially known for its production of locomotives, trucks, and weapons. The company has a rich history and continues to operate in various industries to this day.
