Zvíkov Castle is situated at the confluence of the Vltava and Otava rivers in the Czech Republic. This strategic location has played a significant role in the castle's history, providing natural defenses and controlling trade routes in the region. The castle's position overlooking these rivers made it an important fortress and administrative center during medieval times. The confluence of the Vltava and Otava also adds to the scenic beauty of the area, making Zvíkov Castle a popular destination for tourists interested in history and nature alike.
