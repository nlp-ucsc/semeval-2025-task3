The theater founded by Šlitr, Suchý, and Havlík is called Divadlo Semafor (Semafor Theater). It was established in 1959 in Prague, Czechia, and is known for its unique approach to cabaret, blending music, theater, and humor. Jiří Suchý, Ferdinand Havlík, and Sláva Šlitr were key figures in creating its distinctive style.
