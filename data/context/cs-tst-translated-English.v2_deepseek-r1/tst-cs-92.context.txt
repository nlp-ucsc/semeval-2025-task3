Divoká Šárka, a nature reserve in Prague, Czech Republic, is named after a legendary female figure named Šárka. According to Czech legend, Šárka was a female warrior who, along with other women, rebelled against men during the time of the Maidens' War. She is often associated with the area where she is said to have led her female warriors. The name "Divoká Šárka" translates to "Wild Šárka," reflecting the wild and untamed nature of the region.
