The robber character from books associated with the town of Jičín is **Robin Hood**. Robin Hood is a legendary outlaw known for stealing from the rich to give to the poor, and he is often associated with Sherwood Forest in England. However, in the Czech Republic, there is a famous character named **Robinsonka** from the book "The Adventures of Robin Hood" (Czech: "Dobrodružství Robina Hooda") by Daniela S. Puls, which is set in the area around Jičín. This character is a local version of Robin Hood and is celebrated in the region.
