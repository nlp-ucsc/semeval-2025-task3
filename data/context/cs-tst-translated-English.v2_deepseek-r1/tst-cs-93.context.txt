In the film "Pelíšky" (translated as "Cozy Dens"), the character Saša Mašlano was played by actor Ondřej Brousek. Released in 1999, the film is a Czech coming-of-age comedy-drama directed by Jan Hřebejk. It explores themes of adolescence, family, and social dynamics in the late 1960s. Ondřej Brousek's portrayal of Saša Mašlano is memorable and contributes to the film's nuanced storytelling.
