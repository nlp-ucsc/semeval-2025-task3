The professional football club based in Žižkov, Prague is SK Slavia Prague. However, it's important to note that SK Slavia Prague is primarily associated with the Vršovice district, where their stadium, Eden Arena, is located. Žižkov itself is home to another club, FK Viktoria Žižkov, which has a rich history in Czech football and has played in the top-tier Czech First League in the past, although they currently compete in lower divisions.
