The Czechoslovak animated series featuring the adventures of an oversized talking dog named Fík and a little girl named Ája is called "Maxidog" or "Fík a Ája" in Czech. The series is based on the popular Czech comic strip by Czech cartoonist Ladislav Středa. Created in the 1970s, it follows the heartwarming and humorous relationship between Fík, a giant dog, and Ája, a clever and resourceful little girl, as they get into various adventures and solve problems together. The show was well-loved in Czechoslovakia and remains a nostalgic favorite among many who grew up with it.
