Terrazzo, a municipality in the Province of Verona in the Italian region of Veneto, borders the following nine municipalities[1][2]:

1. Badia Polesine
2. Bevilacqua
3. Boschi Sant'Anna
4. Castagnaro
5. Castelbaldo
6. Legnago
7. Merlara
8. Urbana
9. Villa Bartolomea

These municipalities surround Terrazzo, creating its administrative boundaries within the Veneto region. Terrazzo itself is located about 80 kilometers (50 miles) southwest of Venice and approximately 45 kilometers (28 miles) southeast of Verona, the provincial capital[1][2].
