Chun Jung-myung is South Korean[1][2][3]. He was born on November 29, 1980 in South Korea[1][2][3]. Chun is a well-known South Korean actor who has starred in numerous Korean television dramas and films throughout his career[1][3]. Some of his notable works include TV dramas like "Fashion 70's" (2005), "Goodbye Solo" (2006), "What's Up Fox?" (2006), "Cinderella's Sister" (2010), and "The Master of Revenge" (2016)[3]. He has also appeared in Korean films such as "The Aggressives" (2005), "Hansel and Gretel" (2007), and "Queen of the Night" (2013)[3].
