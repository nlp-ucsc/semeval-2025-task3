Altadena is located in the state of California, United States[1][7]. It is an unincorporated census-designated place situated in Los Angeles County, approximately 14 miles northeast of downtown Los Angeles and directly north of the city of Pasadena[1][7].

Some key details about Altadena's location and population:

1. It is part of the San Gabriel Valley and Verdugos regions of Los Angeles County[7].

2. As of the 2020 census, Altadena had a population of 42,846[1][7].

3. The community covers a total area of about 8.7 square miles (23 km²), with over 99% of it being land[7].

4. Altadena is situated at the foothills of the San Gabriel Mountains, between the Arroyo Seco and Eaton Canyon[5].

5. Its elevation is approximately 1,358 feet (414 meters) above sea level[1].

6. The geographic coordinates of Altadena are 34°11'19"N latitude and 118°8'5"W longitude[1][3].

Altadena's location in California contributes to its Mediterranean climate and diverse landscape, making it a unique community within the greater Los Angeles area[7].
