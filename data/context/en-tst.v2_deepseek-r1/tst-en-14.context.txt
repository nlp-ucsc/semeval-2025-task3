To determine the factors of 150, we'll start by breaking it down into its prime factors:

150 can be divided by 2, resulting in 75.
75 can be divided by 3, resulting in 25.
25 is 5 squared.

So, the prime factors of 150 are 2, 3, and 5 squared.

Using these prime factors, we can list all the factors of 150:
1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 10, 15, 25, 30, 50, 75, and 150.

These are all the numbers that divide 150 without leaving a remainder.
