West Athens is a region within Athens, Greece, and it comprises several municipalities. These include:

1. **Aigaleo** (Αιγάλεω)
2. **Chaidari** (Χαϊδάρι)
3. **Peristeri** (Περιστέρι)
4. **Petroupoli** (Πετρούπολη)
5. **Ilion** (Ίλιον)
6. **Kamatero** (Καματερό)

These municipalities are part of the larger Athens urban area and each has its own unique character and administrative functions.
