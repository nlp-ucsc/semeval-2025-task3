The film "Faster, Pussycat! Kill! Kill!" was written by Russ Meyer and his co-screenwriter Jack Moran. The movie, released in 1965, is a cult classic known for its over-the-top performances, campy dialogue, and action-packed plot. It is often celebrated for its portrayal of strong female characters and has become a staple of exploitation cinema. Russ Meyer, a prominent figure in the sexploitation and camp genres, directed and produced the film as well.
