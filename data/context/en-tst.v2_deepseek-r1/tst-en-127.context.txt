The Infiniti G Line of cars is manufactured by Nissan Motor Co., Ltd. Infiniti is the luxury vehicle division of Nissan, and the G Line was a series of luxury cars produced from 1990 to 2015. These vehicles were known for their blend of performance and luxury, and they played a significant role in establishing Infiniti as a major player in the premium automotive market. The G Line included models such as the G20, G35, G37, and Q40. In 2016, Infiniti rebranded some of its models, and the G Line was succeeded by the Q50.
