The Amanda Show contains a total of 46 episodes across 3 seasons[1][3][5]. Here's a breakdown of the episodes by season:

1. Season 1: 13 episodes, aired from October 16, 1999 to February 19, 2000[1][3][5].

2. Season 2: 17 episodes, aired from July 15, 2000 to April 7, 2001[1][3][5].

3. Season 3: 10 episodes, aired from January 19, 2002 to September 21, 2002[1][3][5].

Additionally, after the main series concluded, 6 "The Best of..." episodes were produced and aired between March 23, 2002 and May 18, 2002[1][5]. These compilation episodes featured highlights from the show's run.

In total, including the main episodes and the "Best of" specials, there are 52 episodes of The Amanda Show. However, when referring to the regular series run, the count is typically given as 46 episodes across 3 seasons.
