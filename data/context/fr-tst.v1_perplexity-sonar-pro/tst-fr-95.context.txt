Wheein released her second mini-album titled "WHEE" on January 16, 2022[1][2][3]. This was her first release under The L1ve label after leaving RBW[4]. The album contains 6 tracks, including the lead single "Make Me Happy"[2][4]. Some key details about the album:

- It was released in two versions: WEST and EAST[1]
- The physical album was released on January 17, 2022[5]
- It debuted at number 4 on the Circle Weekly Album Chart[4]
- It sold over 81,000 copies[4]
- The album contents included a photobook, CD, photocards, and other items[1][5]

"WHEE" marked Wheein's return as a solo artist after her debut EP "Redd" in 2021. The album showcased her musical growth and established her further as a solo performer outside of her work with Mamamoo.
