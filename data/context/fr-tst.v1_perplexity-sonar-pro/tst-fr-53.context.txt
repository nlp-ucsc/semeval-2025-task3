Daniel Edward Lungren was elected to the United States House of Representatives in 1979[1][2]. He represented a district that included portions of Long Beach and Orange County in California[1]. Lungren served in the House of Representatives from 1979 to 1989 during his first stint in Congress[1][5]. He was elected after an unsuccessful run in 1976, winning the seat in 1978 to begin serving in 1979[2]. This marked the start of Lungren's career as an elected official at the federal level[1].
