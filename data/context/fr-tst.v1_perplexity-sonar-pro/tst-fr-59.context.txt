The album "The Eternal Idol" by Black Sabbath contains the following 9 tracks:

1. The Shining
2. Ancient Warrior
3. Hard Life to Love
4. Glory Ride
5. Born to Lose
6. Nightmare
7. Scarlet Pimpernel
8. Lost Forever
9. Eternal Idol

This album, released in 1987, was the thirteenth studio album by Black Sabbath. It was the first to feature vocalist Tony Martin, who would go on to have a significant tenure with the band. The album marked a return to a more traditional heavy metal sound for Black Sabbath after some of their more experimental releases in the mid-1980s.
