Beauvechain is located in the Walloon Brabant province of Belgium. It borders these four communes, with Grez-Doiceau, Incourt, and Jodoigne being in the Walloon region, while Hoegaarden is in the Flemish region[3][5]. 

It's worth noting that Beauvechain's northern border forms part of the linguistic border between the French-speaking Wallonia and Dutch-speaking Flanders[3]. This geographic position makes Beauvechain an interesting point of cultural and linguistic transition in Belgium.
