The Kaiser Chiefs' album "Education, Education, Education and War" was recorded at The Maze Studio in Atlanta, Georgia[1][7][8]. The album was produced by Ben H. Allen III, who has previously worked with artists like Gnarls Barkley, Animal Collective, and Deerhunter[1]. After recording at The Maze Studio, the album was mixed by Michael Brauer at Electric Lady Studios in New York City in November 2013[1][7].
