Based on the search results, Frances Farmer had:

- 1 older sister named Edith
- 1 older brother named Wesley 
- 1 older half-sister named Rita (from her mother's first marriage)

In total, Frances Farmer had 3 siblings: 2 full siblings (Edith and Wesley) and 1 half-sibling (Rita)[1][2][4].

The search results also mention that before Wesley and Edith were born, Frances' mother had given birth to a daughter who died of pneumonia in infancy[2]. However, this child is not typically counted as one of Frances' siblings since she passed away before Frances was born.
