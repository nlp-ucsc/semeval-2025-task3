The Rosaceae, commonly known as the rose family, is a large and diverse family of flowering plants. As of recent classifications, it comprises approximately 95 to 100 genera and around 2,800 to 3,000 species. This family includes a wide range of plants, from shrubs and trees to herbaceous perennials, such as roses, apples, pears, cherries, plums, raspberries, blackberries, and many others. The exact numbers can vary slightly depending on the source and ongoing taxonomic research.
