The President of Vietnam is the head of state and the head of the armed forces. The President is elected by the National Assembly from among its members for a five-year term. As of 2021, the President of Vietnam is Nguyễn Xuân Phúc, who was elected in April 2021. Therefore, the term of the seventh President of Vietnam, Nguyễn Xuân Phúc, is expected to end in 2026.
