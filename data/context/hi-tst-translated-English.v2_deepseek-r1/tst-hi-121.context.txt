The 'Tower of Silence' is located in Mumbai, India. Specifically, it is situated in the Malabar Hill area, which is one of the most prestigious localities in Mumbai. This structure is part of the tradition of the Parsi community, where the dead are placed in towers to be exposed to vultures, following their religious beliefs about the sanctity of earth and fire. The Towers of Silence in Mumbai are among the most well-known and serve as an important cultural and historical site for the Parsi community in India.
