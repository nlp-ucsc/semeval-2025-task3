Alborz Province in Iran is divided into 6 counties (shahrestans). These counties are:

1. Karaj County
2. Savojbolagh County
3. Taleqan County
4. Chaharbagh County
5. Nazarabad County
6. Eshtehard County

Each of these counties is further divided into districts (bakhshs). For the most accurate and up-to-date information, it's best to refer to official sources or local administrative records.
