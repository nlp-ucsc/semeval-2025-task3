One yottagram is equal to \(1 \times 10^{24}\) grams. 

To convert grams to tons, we know that:
- \(1\) kilogram (kg) = \(1,000\) grams
- \(1\) metric ton = \(1,000\) kilograms = \(1,000,000\) grams

Therefore, to convert yottagrams to tons:
- \(1\) yottagram = \(1 \times 10^{24}\) grams
- \(1 \times 10^{24}\) grams รท \(1,000,000\) grams per ton = \(1 \times 10^{18}\) tons

So, there are \(1 \times 10^{18}\) tons in one yottagram.
