The father of Count Carlo Gozzi was Count Francesco Gozzi. Count Carlo Gozzi was known as an Italian dramatist and was also a prominent figure in the Accademia dei Granelleschi, a literary society. He is best known for his plays, particularly in the genre of "fiabe," which are dramatic fairy tales. His works include "La donna serpente" (The Serpent Woman) and "L'amore delle tre melarance" (The Love of Three Oranges). Both of these plays have been adapted into operas by Russian composer Sergei Prokofiev.
