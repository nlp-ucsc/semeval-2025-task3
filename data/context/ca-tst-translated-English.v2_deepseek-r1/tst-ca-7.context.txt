The Greek island of **Angistri** (also spelled Angistri or Anguistri) belongs to the **Saronic Islands** group. These islands are located in the Saronic Gulf, southwest of Athens and north of the Peloponnese. The Saronic Islands are a popular destination for tourists due to their proximity to Athens and their beautiful beaches and charming villages. Angistri is one of the smaller islands in this group, known for its peaceful atmosphere and natural beauty.
