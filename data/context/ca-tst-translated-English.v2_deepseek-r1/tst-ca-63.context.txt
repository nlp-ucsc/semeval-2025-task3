The Fratellis are a Scottish rock band formed in 2005. The original members are:

1. **Jon Fratelli** (lead vocals, guitar) - born John Lawler
2. **Barry Fratelli** (bass guitar, backing vocals) - born Barry Wallace
3. **Mince Fratelli** (drums, percussion, backing vocals) - born Gordon McRory

They are known for their catchy, indie-rock sound and hits like "Chelsea Dagger" and "Flathead."
