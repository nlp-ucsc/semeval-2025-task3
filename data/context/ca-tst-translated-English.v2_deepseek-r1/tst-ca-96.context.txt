The Sweet Chestnut tree, known scientifically as *Castanea sativa*, is also referred to by other names. Some of its common names include:

1. **Chestnut**: This is a widely used term, though it can sometimes refer specifically to the Sweet Chestnut.
2. **Spanish Chestnut**: This name is often used in certain regions to distinguish it from other chestnut species.
3. **European Chestnut**: Since it is native to Europe, this name helps identify its origin.
4. **Chestnut Tree**: A straightforward name that emphasizes its tree form.

These names are often used interchangeably depending on the region and context.
