Catherine McCormack was born in Epsom, Surrey, England, UK[1][4]. She was born on April 3, 1972[1]. 

While some sources incorrectly list her birthplace as Alton, Hampshire[2][3], the most authoritative sources, including her IMDb biography, clearly state that she was born in Epsom, Surrey. Epsom is a market town in Surrey, England, located about 15 miles southwest of central London.

It's worth noting that there is some discrepancy regarding her exact birth date. While most sources, including IMDb, list her birth date as April 3, 1972[1][4], a few other sources incorrectly state January 1, 1972[2][3]. However, the April 3 date appears to be the most accurate based on the majority of reliable sources.
