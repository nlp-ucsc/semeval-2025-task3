The asteroid named after Sergei Parajanov is 3963 Paradzhanov[1][4]. This asteroid was discovered on October 8, 1969, by astronomer Lyudmila Chernykh at the Crimean Astrophysical Observatory[1]. It was officially named after the Soviet film director Sergei Parajanov (also known as Sargis Paradzhanov) in 1996[1].

Some key details about asteroid 3963 Paradzhanov:

1. Classification: It is classified as a Nysian asteroid, belonging to the Nysa family in the inner regions of the asteroid belt[1].
