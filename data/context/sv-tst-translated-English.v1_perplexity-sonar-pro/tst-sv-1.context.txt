David F. Sandberg was born in Jönköping, Sweden[1][2][3]. Jönköping is a city located in southern Sweden. Sandberg began his filmmaking career in his hometown, working at the local film center called Film I Jönköping[1]. He developed his passion for filmmaking at a young age, using his father's VHS camera to make amateur movies[1][4]. Sandberg's Swedish roots and early experiences in Jönköping played a significant role in shaping his career as a filmmaker before he moved to Hollywood and became known for directing successful horror films and superhero movies.
