Gianrico Carofiglio was elected to the Italian Senate in 2022. He won in the Apulia region with the "Noi Moderati" (Us Moderates) party. His term as senator started on October 13, 2022, and he served in this position until his resignation on June 29, 2023. Therefore, he served as a senator for approximately eight months before stepping down from his role in the second chamber.
