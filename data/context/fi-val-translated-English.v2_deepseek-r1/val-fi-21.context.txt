The Swedish Navy possesses a variety of weapons to fulfill both offensive and defensive roles. These include:

1. Submarines: Equipped with torpedoes and antimissile weapons.
2. Corvette Warships: Fitted with guns, missiles, and torpedoes.
3. Mine Countermeasure Vessels: Utilize systems for mine detection and neutralization.
4. Patrol Boats: Armed with machine guns and sometimes guided missiles.

This armament allows the Swedish Navy to engage in numerous operations, ensuring national security and contributing to international peacekeeping efforts.
