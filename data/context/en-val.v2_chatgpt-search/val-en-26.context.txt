Yes, the Arts and Humanities Citation Index (AHCI) is still actively maintained. It is a multidisciplinary index covering over 1,700 arts and humanities journals, as well as selected items from more than 250 scientific and social sciences journals, with coverage dating back to 1975. [MJL CLARIVATE] AHCI is part of the Web of Science Core Collection, which is regularly updated to ensure comprehensive and current coverage of scholarly literature.
