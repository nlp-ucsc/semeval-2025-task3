Chance the Rapper, born Chancelor Johnathan Bennett on April 16, 1993, in Chicago, Illinois, began his music career in 2011 during his senior year at Jones College Prep High School. Following a 10-day suspension for marijuana possession, he utilized this time to record his debut mixtape, 10 Day, which he released independently on April 3, 2012. The mixtape garnered significant attention, leading to his breakthrough with the 2013 release of his second mixtape, Acid Rap.
