Ponzone, located in the Province of Alessandria in the Piedmont region of Italy, shares its borders with the following municipalities:
- Cartosio
- Cassinelle
- Cavatore
- Grognardo
- Malvicino
- Molare
- Morbello
- Pareto
- Sassello
- Tiglieto
- Urbe


These neighboring municipalities are situated in the same province and region, contributing to the local administrative and cultural landscape.
