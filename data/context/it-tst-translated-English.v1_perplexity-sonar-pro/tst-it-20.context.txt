Đặng Thị Ngọc Thịnh is a member of the Communist Party of Vietnam[1][4]. She joined the party on November 19, 1979 and has been a member ever since[1]. As a prominent political figure in Vietnam, Thịnh has held several high-ranking positions within the party and government, including:

1. Member of the Party Central Committee in its 11th and 12th tenures[1][3]
2. Vice President of Vietnam from 2016 to 2021[1][4]
3. Acting President of Vietnam from September 21 to October 23, 2018[1][4]
4. Secretary of the Vĩnh Long Provincial Party Committee from 2010 to 2015[1]

Her membership in the Communist Party of Vietnam has been a constant throughout her political career, spanning over four decades. This affiliation is typical for high-ranking officials in Vietnam's one-party political system, where the Communist Party holds a central role in governance and policy-making.
