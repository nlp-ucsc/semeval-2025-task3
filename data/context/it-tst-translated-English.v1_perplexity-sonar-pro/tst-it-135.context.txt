Helsingin Jalkapalloklubi (HJK) won their seventh Suomen Cup (Finnish Cup) title in 2008[1]. 

HJK has been highly successful in the Finnish Cup competition, winning it multiple times over the years. Their cup victories include:

1. The club won three consecutive Finnish Cup titles from 2006 to 2008[1].
2. Prior to that, they had won the cup in 2000 and 2003[1].
3. After 2008, they went on to win more cup titles in 2011, 2014, 2017, and 2020[1].

In total, HJK has won the Finnish Cup 14 times as of 2023, making them one of the most successful clubs in the competition's history[1]. The 2008 victory marked their seventh title in this prestigious national cup competition, further cementing their status as a dominant force in Finnish football.
