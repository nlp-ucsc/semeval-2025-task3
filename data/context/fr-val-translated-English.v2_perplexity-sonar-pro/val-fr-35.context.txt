Nora Fatehi is a Canadian citizen. She was born on February 6, 1992 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada and was raised in Toronto[1][4]. While she has Moroccan heritage from both her father's and mother's side, her nationality is Canadian[2][3]. 

Some key points about Nora Fatehi's background:

- She was born and raised in Canada to parents of Moroccan descent[1][4]
- She completed her early education at Westview Centennial Secondary School in Toronto[2][3]
- She later studied at York University in Toronto, though she had to drop out before completing her degree[2][3]
- She is fluent in English, Hindi, Arabic and French[3]

Despite her Canadian nationality, Nora Fatehi has established a successful career in the Indian film industry, particularly Bollywood. She has stated in some interviews that she considers herself "an Indian at heart"[1]. However, in terms of official citizenship and nationality, she remains Canadian.
