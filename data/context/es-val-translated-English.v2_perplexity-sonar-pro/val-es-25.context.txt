Ponzone is located in Italy[1][2][4]. Specifically, it is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Alessandria in the Piedmont region of Italy[1][4]. Ponzone is situated about 80 kilometers (50 miles) southeast of Turin and approximately 40 kilometers (25 miles) southwest of Alessandria[6].
