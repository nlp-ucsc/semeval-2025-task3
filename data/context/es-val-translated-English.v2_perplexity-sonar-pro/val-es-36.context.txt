Valrico is a census-designated place (CDP) located in Hillsborough County, Florida, United States[1][3]. Here are some key details about its location:

1. Geographic coordinates: Valrico is situated at 27°56'27"N 82°14'33"W (27.940774, -82.242551)[1].

2. Surrounding areas:
   - It is bordered to the west by Brandon
   - To the southwest by Bloomingdale
   - To the south by FishHawk[1]

3. Proximity to major cities:
   - It is approximately 14 miles (23 km) east of Tampa
   - 26 miles (42 km) west of Bartow
   - 10 miles (16 km) southwest of Plant City[1]

4. Size and topography:
   - The Valrico CDP has a total area of 14.2 square miles (36.8 km²)
   - Of this, 13.8 square miles (35.8 km²) is land
   - 0.42 square miles (1.1 km²), or 2.92%, is water[1]

5. Notable features:
   - Valrico Lake, a 124-acre lake, is part of the Pemberton Creek/Baker Canal Watershed in the area[2]
   - The area is part of a natural drainage system that eventually connects to the Gulf of Mexico[2]

Valrico is primarily a suburban community that has grown significantly since the mid-20th century, largely due to its proximity to Tampa and its location along major thoroughfares like State Road 60[1].
