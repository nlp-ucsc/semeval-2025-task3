HSC Csíkszereda currently plays in the Erste Liga, which is an international ice hockey league for clubs based in Hungary and Romania[1][2][4]. 

Some key details about HSC Csíkszereda's participation in the Erste Liga:

1. The team is one of the most successful in the league's history, having won 3 titles, which ties them with DVTK Jegesmedvék for the most championships[2].

2. In the 2024-2025 season, HSC Csíkszereda has played 34 games so far, with a record of 12 wins, 17 losses, 3 overtime wins, and 2 overtime losses[3].

3. The team is currently ranked 7th in the Erste Liga standings for the 2024-2025 season[3].

4. HSC Csíkszereda has been a consistent performer in recent years, winning back-to-back championships in the 2020-2021 and 2021-2022 seasons[2][3].

5. In addition to the Erste Liga, HSC Csíkszereda also participates in the Romanian national championship, where they have also been successful[3][4].

The Erste Liga, formerly known as the MOL Liga, was founded in 2008 and currently features 10 teams - 7 from Hungary and 3 from Romania[4]. It serves as the top-tier professional ice hockey league for both countries, with games between Hungarian teams also counting towards the Hungarian National Championship (OB I Bajnokság)[4].
