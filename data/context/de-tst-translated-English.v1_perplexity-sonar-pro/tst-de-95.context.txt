Milica Mandić of Serbia won the gold medal in the women's heavyweight (+67 kg) taekwondo competition at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London[5]. She defeated Anne-Caroline Graffe of France in the final match to claim the gold medal[5][8]. 

The full podium for this event was:

1. Gold: Milica Mandić (Serbia)
2. Silver: Anne-Caroline Graffe (France)
3. Bronze: María Espinoza (Mexico)
3. Bronze: Anastasiya Baryshnikova (Russia)[5]

This was Serbia's first gold medal in taekwondo at the Olympic Games. The competition took place on August 11, 2012 at the ExCeL Exhibition Centre in London[1][3].
