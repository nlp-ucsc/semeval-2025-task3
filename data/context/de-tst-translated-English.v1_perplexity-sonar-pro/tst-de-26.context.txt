In the summer of 2007, Mario Bolatti transferred to FC Porto from Belgrano[2][7].

Specifically:

- Bolatti began his career at Belgrano in 2003[2].
- In July 2007, he was transferred to Portuguese club Porto, where he signed a four-year contract[2].
- Porto acquired 60% of Bolatti's economic rights for future transfer fees as part of this deal[2].
- The UEFA website confirms that Porto added Bolatti to their squad in July 2007, describing him as a 22-year-old Argentinian who signed from Belgrano[7].

This transfer marked Bolatti's first move to a European club after starting his professional career in Argentina with Belgrano. It was a significant step in his career, joining a prominent Portuguese club that regularly competes in European competitions.
