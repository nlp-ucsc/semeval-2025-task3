The municipality of Beauvechain in Belgium consists of the following districts:

1. Beauvechain
2. Hamme-Mille 
3. L'Écluse
4. Nodebais
5. Tourinnes-la-Grosse[1]

Beauvechain is located in the province of Walloon Brabant in the Wallonia region of Belgium. The total area of the municipality is 38.58 km², with a population of 6,529 inhabitants as of January 1, 2006, resulting in a population density of 169 inhabitants per km²[1].

Beauvechain is notable for being home to Beauvechain Air Base, a Belgian Air Component military airfield located about 3 nautical miles (5.6 km) south of the town[2]. The air base houses the 1st Wing, which operates A109BA helicopters, as well as the Basic Flying Training School that uses SIAI Marchetti SF.260 trainer aircraft[2].
