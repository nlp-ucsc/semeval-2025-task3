The aircraft known as the DHC-8 or Dash-8 series are produced by Bombardier Aerospace. The primary production facilities for these aircraft are located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Specifically, the manufacturing takes place at the Downsview Airport, which has been a major site for aircraft production for many years. Bombardier has a long history of producing various aircraft models, including the Dash-8 series, which are widely used for regional and commuter flights.
