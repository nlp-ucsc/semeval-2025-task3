Catherine McCormack has acted in over 40 films and television shows throughout her career. Among her most notable roles, three stand out:

1. **Braveheart (1995)** - She played the role of Murron MacClannough, the wife of William Wallace, in this epic historical drama directed by Mel Gibson.

2. **The Land Girls (1998)** - McCormack starred alongside Rachel Weisz and Anna Friel in this film about women working on a British farm during World War II.

3. **Spy Game (2001)** - She played the role of Elizabeth Hadley, the love interest of Tom Bishop (Brad Pitt), in this espionage thriller starring Robert Redford.
