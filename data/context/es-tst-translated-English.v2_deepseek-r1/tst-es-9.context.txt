Sophie Nélisse played the role of Liesel Meminger in the 2013 movie "The Book Thief" directed by Brian Percival. The film is based on the best-selling novel of the same name by Markus Zusak. In the story, Liesel is a young girl in Nazi Germany during World War II who discovers the power of words and literature. Sophie Nélisse's performance as Liesel was well-received and marked a significant role in her acting career.
