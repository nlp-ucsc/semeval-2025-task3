The American movie "Sweet Home Alabama" (2002), starring Reese Witherspoon, was honored with several awards and nominations. Some notable recognitions include:

1. **Teen Choice Awards (2003)**: The film won the Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie: Drama.

2. **People's Choice Awards (2003)**: Reese Witherspoon won the People's Choice Award for Favorite Comedic Movie Actress for her role in the film.

3. **MTV Movie & TV Awards (2003)**: The movie was nominated for Best Female Performance for Reese Witherspoon's portrayal of Melanie Carmichael. Although she didn't win in that category, the film's success was acknowledged with this nomination.

These awards highlight the film's popularity and the standout performance of its lead actress. Additionally, "Sweet Home Alabama" was a commercial success, grossing over $180 million worldwide.
