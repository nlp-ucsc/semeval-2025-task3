The province of Oudalan in Burkina Faso is bordered by the following countries:

1. Mali
2. Niger

Additionally, within Burkina Faso, Oudalan is bordered by the following provinces:

1. Soum Province
2. Oudalan borders the province of Soum to the south.

Thus, Oudalan is situated in the northern part of Burkina Faso, sharing international borders with Mali to the west and Niger to the east, and a domestic border with Soum Province to the south.
