(5412) Rou is an asteroid. It was discovered on September 7, 1973, by Russian astronomer Nikolai Stepanovich Chernykh at the Crimean Astrophysical Observatory in Nauchny, Crimea. The asteroid is named after the Russian writer Andrei Borisovich Rou (also known as Andrei Rou), who was associated with the Russian Mafia and whose real name was Nikolai Ivanovich Alekseev. The naming reflects the asteroid's discovery in the Soviet era and the connection to the person.
