The main rival of HJK Helsinki is HIFK Helsinki. The two teams have a long-standing rivalry, often referred to as the "Stadin derby," which is one of the most famous football derbies in Finland. HJK and HIFK are both based in Helsinki, leading to a fierce local rivalry that spans many sports, but football is particularly significant. Matches between these two teams are highly anticipated and often draw large crowds and significant media attention.
