In the American film "Faster, Pussycat! Kill! Kill!", the three main protagonists are:

1. Varla
2. Rosie
3. Billie

They are part of a go-go dance trio who go on a violent spree in the desert. The film, released in 1965, is directed by Russ Meyer and has become a cult classic known for its over-the-top action and feminist themes.
