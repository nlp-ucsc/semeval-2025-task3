The game F.E.A.R., developed by Monolith Productions, has had a few sequels and expansions. The mainline sequels include F.E.A.R. 2: Project Origin and F.E.A.R. 3. Additionally, there have been expansion packs like F.E.A.R.: Extraction Point and F.E.A.R.: Perseus Mandate. So in total, there are two sequels and two expansions.
