As of the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, Cape Verde has participated in a total of 6 editions of the Olympic Games. The country made its debut at the 1996 Atlanta Olympics and has been participating consistently since then, with the exception of the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics, which it did not attend. Therefore, Cape Verde's participation includes the Summer Olympics from 1996 to 2020 and one Winter Olympics appearance in 2014 in Sochi, Russia.
