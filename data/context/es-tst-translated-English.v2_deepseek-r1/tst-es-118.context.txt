The 2018 Chinese Grand Prix was held on April 15, 2018, at the Shanghai International Circuit. The race was won by Daniel Ricciardo of Red Bull Racing. It was a thrilling race with Ricciardo making a series of overtakes after starting from sixth place, and a mid-race safety car period played a significant role in his victory. Mercedes' Valtteri Bottas and Ferrari's Kimi Räikkönen completed the podium in second and third places, respectively.
