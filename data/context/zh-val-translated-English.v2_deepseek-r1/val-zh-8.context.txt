The city of Delta in the province of British Columbia, Canada, is bordered by several neighboring cities and municipalities. To the north, Delta is adjacent to Surrey. To the northeast, it shares a border with the City of New Westminster. To the east and southeast, Delta is bordered by the city of Surrey again. To the south, it meets the waters of the Strait of Georgia, which is part of the Salish Sea. Delta also shares its northwest boundary with the city of Richmond, across the Fraser River. Lastly, to the west, Delta is bordered by the Municipality of Bowen Island. This strategic location places Delta within a vibrant urban and natural landscape.
