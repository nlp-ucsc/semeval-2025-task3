Susan Mayer, played by Teri Hatcher, is one of the main characters in the TV series "Desperate Housewives," which aired from 2004 to 2012. Her family members include her husband Karl Mayer, her daughter Julie Mayer, and her son M.J. Mayer. Throughout the series, Susan's family life is a central part of her storyline, dealing with issues like divorce, remarriage, and raising her children.
