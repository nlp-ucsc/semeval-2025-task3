Jeff Raskin was an American computer scientist and expert in human-computer interaction. He was born in 1943 and passed away in 2005. Raskin was known for his work at Apple, where he is often credited with playing a significant role in the development of the Macintosh computer. 

Regarding his educational background, Jeff Raskin earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Mathematics, a Master of Arts degree in Computer Science, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science, with a minor in Cognitive Psychology, from the University of California, San Diego (UCSD). His academic work laid the foundation for his contributions to user interface design and human-computer interaction.
