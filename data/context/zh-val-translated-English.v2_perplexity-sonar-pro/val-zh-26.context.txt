Chance the Rapper has won several prestigious awards throughout his career:

Grammy Awards:
Chance the Rapper had a breakthrough year at the 59th Annual Grammy Awards in 2017, where he won three awards[2][4]:

1. Best New Artist - He became the first male rap solo artist to win this award[3].
2. Best Rap Album for "Coloring Book" - This was the first streaming-only album to win a Grammy Award[2][3].
3. Best Rap Performance for "No Problem" (featuring Lil Wayne and 2 Chainz)[2].

Other notable awards include:

BET Awards:
- 2017 BET Award winner for Humanitarian Award[1][2].

NAACP Image Awards:
- 2017 Image Award winner for Outstanding New Artist[2].

iHeartRadio Music Awards:
- 2017 winner for Best New Hip-Hop Artist[2].
- 2018 winner of the iHeartRadio Innovator Award[2].

Soul Train Music Awards:
- 2016 winner for Best New Artist[2].

Teen Choice Awards:
- 2017 winner for Choice Breakout Artist[2].
- 2017 winner for Choice Song: R&B/Hip-Hop for "I'm the One" (with DJ Khaled, Justin Bieber, Quavo and Lil Wayne)[2].

Additionally, Chance the Rapper has received numerous nominations for other awards, including American Music Awards, MTV Video Music Awards, and Billboard Music Awards[1][2]. His work has been recognized across various categories, highlighting his impact on hip-hop and the music industry as a whole.
