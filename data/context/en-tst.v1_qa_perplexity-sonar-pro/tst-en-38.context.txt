The name of Folorunsho Alakija's foundation is not the Alafalefa Foundation. The correct name is the Rose of Sharon Foundation. 

Folorunsho Alakija established the Rose of Sharon Foundation on May 23, 2008[7]. This foundation is dedicated to helping widows, their children, and orphans[1][7]. The Rose of Sharon Foundation provides support through various programs including vocational training, interest-free loans, housing assistance, medical care, and educational scholarships[7]. Since its inception, the foundation has directly impacted over 4,262 widows and provided educational scholarships to over 1,971 widows' children and 322 orphans[7].
