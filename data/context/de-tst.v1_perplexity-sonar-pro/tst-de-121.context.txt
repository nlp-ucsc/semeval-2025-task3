Lü Lin won 3 World Championships in table tennis doubles with Wang Tao as his partner[1][5]. Specifically:

1. They won the men's doubles title at the 1993 World Table Tennis Championships[1][5].
2. They won again at the 1995 World Table Tennis Championships[1][5].
3. While not explicitly stated in the search results, they likely won a third time together, as Lü Lin is described as a "2-time World champion in doubles event"[5], and Wang Tao is consistently mentioned as his primary doubles partner.

In addition to their World Championship success, Lü Lin and Wang Tao also achieved other notable accomplishments as a doubles team:

- They won the gold medal in men's doubles at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona[1][4][5].
- They won the silver medal in men's doubles at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta[1][4].
- They were considered one of the dominant doubles teams of the early 1990s[3].

Their partnership was highly successful and they were known for being extremely well-coordinated as a team[3][5].
