The 2011 UEFA Super Cup was contested between FC Barcelona and FC Porto[1]. 

FC Barcelona qualified for the match as the winners of the 2010–11 UEFA Champions League, while FC Porto qualified as the winners of the 2010–11 UEFA Europa League[1].

The match took place on 26 August 2011 at the Stade Louis II in Monaco[1]. Barcelona won the title by defeating Porto 2–0, with goals from Lionel Messi in the 39th minute and Cesc Fàbregas in the 88th minute[1][5].

This was Barcelona's 8th appearance in the UEFA Super Cup, having previously participated in 1979, 1982, 1989, 1992, 1997, 2006, and 2009[1]. For Porto, it was their 4th appearance, having previously played in the Super Cup in 1987, 2003, and 2004[1].
