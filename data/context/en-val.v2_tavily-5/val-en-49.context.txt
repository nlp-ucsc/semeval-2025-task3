Count Carl Löwenhielm was a Swedish military officer, diplomat, and politician; he was a member of the Swedish cabinet between 1822-1839. Career He was an illegitimate son of King Charles XIII of Sweden and Augusta von Fersen (married to the Chancellor of the Royal Court Fredrik Adolf Löwenhielm) and a half-brother of Gustaf Löwenhielm.
