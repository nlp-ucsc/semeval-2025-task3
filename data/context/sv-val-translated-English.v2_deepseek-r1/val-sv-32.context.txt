Al-Khobar is located in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia, along the Persian Gulf. It is part of the Dammam metropolitan area, which also includes Dammam and Dhahran. Al-Khobar is a major city in the region and is known for its economic significance, particularly in the oil and gas industry. It is situated approximately 13 kilometers (8 miles) southwest of Dammam and about 350 kilometers (217 miles) northeast of Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia. The city is a hub for both business and tourism, with a mix of modern infrastructure and cultural attractions.
