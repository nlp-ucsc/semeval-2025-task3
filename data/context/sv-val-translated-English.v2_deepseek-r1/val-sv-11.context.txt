Hamilton Academical FC, often abbreviated as Hamilton Accies, is a Scottish football club based in Hamilton, South Lanarkshire. The club was founded in 1874.

Their first match was against a local team called Hamilton Rovers, which took place on August 5, 1876. The match ended in a 1-0 victory for Hamilton Academical.

It's worth noting that the club has a rich history in Scottish football, having won several regional and national competitions over the years, including the Scottish Football League First Division (now the Scottish Championship) and the Scottish Challenge Cup. They have also spent several seasons in the top-tier Scottish Premiership.
