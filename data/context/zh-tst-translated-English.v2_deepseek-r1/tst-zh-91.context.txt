The major actors in the 2007 movie "Hitman" include Timothy Olyphant as the main character Agent 47, Dougray Scott as Mike Whittier, Olga Kurylenko as Nika Boronina, Robert Knepper as Yuri Marklov, and Ulrich Thomsen as Mikhail Belicoff. The film is based on the popular video game series of the same name and follows the story of a highly skilled assassin known as Agent 47.
