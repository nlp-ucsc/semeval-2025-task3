The West Athens Regional Unit is a regional unit in the Attica region of Greece. It was established in the 2011 Kallikratis Plan. Here are the municipalities that make up the West Athens Regional Unit:

1. **Egaleo**
2. **Agia Varvara**
3. **Chaidari**
4. **Peristeri**
5. **Petroupoli**

These municipalities were previously part of the Athens Prefecture before the administrative reforms.
