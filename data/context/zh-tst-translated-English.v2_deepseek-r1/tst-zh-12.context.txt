Domantas Sabonis has played for the following teams throughout his basketball career:

1. **Oklahoma City Thunder** (2016–2017)
2. **Indiana Pacers** (2017–2022)
3. **Sacramento Kings** (2022–present)

Sabonis was initially drafted by the Orlando Magic but was traded to the Thunder on draft night. He has since established himself as a strong player in the NBA, known for his versatility and physical play.
