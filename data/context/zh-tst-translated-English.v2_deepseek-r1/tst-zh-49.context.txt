The *Aston Martin V12 Vanquish* is equipped with a 5.9-liter V12 engine, which delivers impressive performance. It produces approximately *568 horsepower* and *458 lb-ft of torque*. This powerful engine allows the car to accelerate from *0 to 60 mph in just 3.6 seconds* and reach a *top speed of 201 mph*. The V12's smooth power delivery and rich exhaust note contribute to a thrilling driving experience.
