The Cuban national men's volleyball team has won several awards. They won the gold medal at the 1976 Montreal Olympics and the 1980 Moscow Olympics. Additionally, they have claimed victory three times in the Men's Volleyball World Championship, taking home the title in 1977, 1989, and 1990. The team has also triumphed in the FIVB Volleyball Men's World Cup, securing first place in 1989, 1991, and 1995. Furthermore, they have celebrated success in the NORCECA Volleyball Championship, winning the championship six times between 1981 and 1997.
