University College Cork, also known as UCC and National University of Ireland, Cork, is divided into four colleges:

1. **College of Arts, Celtic Studies & Social Sciences**
2. **College of Business & Law**
3. **College of Medicine & Health**
4. **College of Science, Engineering & Food Science**
