Kyle Korver had a long and accomplished NBA career, playing for several teams. Here's a list of the teams he played for:

1. **Philadelphia 76ers** (1999-2007)
2. **Utah Jazz** (2007-2010)
3. **Chicago Bulls** (2010-2012)
4. **Atlanta Hawks** (2012-2017)
5. **Cleveland Cavaliers** (2017-2018)
6. **Utah Jazz** (2018-2019)
7. **Phoenix Suns** (2019)
8. **Brooklyn Nets** (2020)

Notably, he also played overseas before joining the NBA. Korver was known for his three-point shooting and had a successful career as a sharpshooter.
