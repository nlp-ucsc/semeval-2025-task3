Since 2000, Helsingin Jalkapalloklubi, commonly known as HJK Helsinki, has been playing its home games at the Bolt Arena in Helsinki, Finland. Bolt Arena is also often referred to by its former name, Sonera Stadium. The stadium was opened in 2000 and has a seating capacity of approximately 10,770 spectators. It serves as a state-of-the-art facility for football matches and has hosted numerous important games, including those in the Finnish Premier Division (Veikkausliiga) and European competitions. Bolt Arena is well-known for its excellent playing surface and has become a significant venue for football in Finland.
