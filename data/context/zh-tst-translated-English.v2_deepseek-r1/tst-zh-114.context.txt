La Balme-de-Sillingy is a commune located in the Haute-Savoie department in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region of France. The municipalities bordering La Balme-de-Sillingy include:

1. **Sillingy** - Located to the north.
2. **Groisy** - Situated to the northeast.
3. **Mésigny** - Lies to the east.
4. **La Roche-sur-Foron** - Positioned to the southeast.
5. **Monnetier-Mornex** - Borders to the south.
6. **Petit-Bornand-les-Glières** - Located to the southwest.
7. **Fillinges** - Situated to the west.
8. **Nangy** - Borders to the northwest.

These municipalities create the communal limits of La Balme-de-Sillingy within the département of Haute-Savoie.
