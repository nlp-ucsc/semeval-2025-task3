The Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC), also known as the Revolutionary Guards, was established after the 1979 Islamic Revolution in Iran. The founder of the IRGC is widely recognized to be Ayatollah Ruhollah Khomeini, the leader of the revolution and the founder of the Islamic Republic of Iran. Khomeini established the IRGC to safeguard the revolution and its ideals, and it has since become a powerful military and political force in Iran.
