Ukraine declared its independence on August 24, 1991. The first countries to recognize Ukraine's independence were:

1. **Canada**: On December 2, 1991, the Canadian government officially recognized Ukraine as an independent state.

2. **Poland**: Poland recognized Ukraine's independence on December 2, 1991, as well.

3. **Lithuania**: Lithuania also extended recognition on December 2, 1991.

4. **Hungary**: Hungary followed suit on December 3, 1991.

These early recognitions were significant in establishing Ukraine's sovereignty on the international stage.
