The Iranian painter recognized as the founder of the Tehran miniature school is Kamal-ol-Molk (Mohammad Ghaffari). He was a renowned artist and educator who played a significant role in reviving and modernizing the Persian miniature tradition in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Kamal-ol-Molk is celebrated for his meticulous attention to detail and his ability to blend traditional Persian art with European techniques, contributing to the development of a distinctive style within the Tehran miniature school.
