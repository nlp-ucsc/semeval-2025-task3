In Iran, the solar calendar is used which is 1443 years ahead of the Gregorian calendar. Therefore, the year 1390 in the Iranian calendar corresponds to 2011-2012 in the Gregorian calendar. Based on the Iranian Census of 2011, the average age of first marriage for men was approximately 26 years old. This figure reflects the rising trend in the marriage age in Iran due to various socio-economic factors, including higher education pursuits and economic challenges.
