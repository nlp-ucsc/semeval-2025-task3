The Shahnameh, also known as The Epic of Kings, is a long epic poem written by the Persian poet Ferdowsi. It is estimated that the Shahnameh contains approximately 50,000 to 60,000 verses, depending on the edition and the counting method. Ferdowsi completed the poem around 1010 AD, after dedicating about 30 years of his life to its composition. The Shahnameh is considered one of the most important works of Persian literature and tells the story of Iran's history, mythology, and legendary heroes.
