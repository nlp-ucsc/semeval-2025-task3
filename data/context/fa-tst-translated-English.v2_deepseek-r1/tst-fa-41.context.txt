The first Iranian novel is widely considered to be "The Adventures of Hajji Baba of Ispahan," written by James Morier in 1824. Although it is an Iranian novel, it was written by a British author. The first Persian-language novel is "Tohfeh-ye Morvarid" by Mirza Aqa Tabrizi, published in 1902. It is considered the first novel in the Persian language.
