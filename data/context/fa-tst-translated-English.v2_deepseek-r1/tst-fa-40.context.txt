The Iranian poet known as the "Tongue of the Unseen" is **Molana Jalal ad-Din Muhammad Balkhi**, more commonly referred to as **Rumi**. Rumi is one of the most celebrated poets in Persian literature and is renowned for his mystical and spiritual verses that explore themes of love, spirituality, and the divine. His works are deeply immersive and have had a profound impact on poetry and philosophy both in the Islamic world and globally.
