Iran has sovereignty over several islands in the Persian Gulf. The exact number can vary based on geographical definitions, but some of the most notable islands include:

1. Kish Island
2. Qeshm Island
3. Hormuz Island
4. Lavan Island
5. Lark Island
6. Hengam Island
7. Abu Musa Island
8. Greater Tunb
9. Lesser Tunb

These islands are under Iranian jurisdiction and are strategic locations in the Persian Gulf.
