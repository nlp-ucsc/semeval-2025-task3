Cilleruelo de San Mamés is a municipality located in the province of Segovia, in the autonomous community of Castile and León, Spain[1][3]. 

Some key details about Cilleruelo de San Mamés:

- It is a small municipality with a population of only 35 inhabitants as of 2024[7].
- The municipality has an area of 9.73 square kilometers[1].
- It sits at an elevation of 982 meters above sea level[1].
- The exact geographical coordinates are latitude 41.4330723 and longitude -3.5654535[2].
- It is located in the Central European Time Zone[2].

Cilleruelo de San Mamés, like other municipalities in the province of Segovia, is part of the larger region of Castile and León in north-central Spain. The province of Segovia is known for its historical sites, including the famous Roman aqueduct in the city of Segovia, as well as its rural landscapes and traditional villages like Cilleruelo de San Mamés.
