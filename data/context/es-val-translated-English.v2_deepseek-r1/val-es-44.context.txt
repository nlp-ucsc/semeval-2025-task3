The main ingredients of chocolate syrup typically include:

1. **Corn Syrup**: This serves as a sweetener and helps in maintaining the syrup's consistency.
2. **Sugar**: Adds sweetness to the syrup.
3. **Cocoa Powder**: Provides the chocolate flavor.
4. **Water**: Thins the syrup to the desired consistency.
5. **Flavorings**: Such as vanilla extract to enhance the taste.
6. **Preservatives**: To extend shelf life.
7. **Salt**: To balance the sweetness.

These ingredients may vary depending on the brand or recipe.
