The football team that plays at the Red Bull Arena in Salzburg is FC Red Bull Salzburg. They are an Austrian professional football club based in Wals-Siezenheim, Salzburg. The stadium, which is also known as the Red Bull Arena, has a seating capacity of around 30,000 and serves as the home ground for FC Red Bull Salzburg, which competes in the Austrian Bundesliga, the top tier of Austrian football.

The club was originally founded in 1933 as SV Austria Salzburg and was later taken over by the Red Bull company in 2005, leading to its rebranding. Since then, the team has enjoyed significant success, including multiple Austrian Bundesliga titles and participation in European competitions like the UEFA Champions League and the UEFA Europa League.

It's worth noting that there is another Red Bull Arena in Leipzig, Germany, which is home to RB Leipzig, another team owned by Red Bull. This can sometimes lead to confusion, so it's important to clarify the location when discussing the Red Bull Arena.
