The comic book series popularized by writer Chris Claremont is the *X-Men*. Claremont's extensive run from 1975 to 1991 is particularly renowned, during which he collaborated with artists like Dave Cockrum and John Byrne. His work on *Uncanny X-Men* and *X-Men* significantly shaped the franchise, introducing iconic characters such as Wolverine, Storm, and Rogue, while also developing complex storylines that explored themes of prejudice and identity. His storytelling had a profound impact on the Marvel Universe and remains influential in the X-Men mythos.
