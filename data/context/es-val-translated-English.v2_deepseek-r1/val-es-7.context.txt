Petra van Staveren is known primarily for her role in the controversial case involving the death of her daughter, Savanna Dekker, in 2009. The case drew significant media attention due to the circumstances surrounding the death and the subsequent legal proceedings. Van Staveren was convicted in the Netherlands of child abandonment, as it was alleged that she left her daughter in a life-threatening situation. The case sparked debates about parenting responsibilities and the legal system's handling of such sensitive cases.
