Sadio Mané is the top scorer of the Senegal national football team[1][3][5]. He has scored 46 goals in 113 appearances for Senegal since his debut in 2012, making him the nation's all-time top goalscorer[1][3]. Mané surpassed the previous record held by Henri Camara, who had 29 goals[5].

Mané has been a key player for Senegal, contributing significantly to their recent successes. He played a crucial role in Senegal's victory at the 2021 Africa Cup of Nations, where he scored the winning penalty in the final against Egypt[3]. He was also named the player of the tournament[3].

In June 2022, Mané scored a hat-trick in a 3-1 win against Benin in the 2023 Africa Cup of Nations qualification, which helped him break the previous goalscoring record[3]. His performances for the national team have earned him recognition as one of the greatest African players of all time[3].
