Wheein debuted as a member of the K-pop girl group MAMAMOO on June 18, 2014[1][3]. MAMAMOO was formed and managed by RBW Entertainment (formerly known as RWB) and consists of four members: Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein, and Hwasa[3]. The group is known for their strong vocal abilities, harmonies, and involvement in writing much of their own material. They have gained recognition as one of the premier girl groups in K-pop, known for their talent in crossing multiple genres including retro, jazz, R&B, hip-hop, and emotional ballads[3].
