The Fratellis are a Scottish rock band from Glasgow, formed in 2005. The primary members of the band are:

1. **Jon Fratelli**: Lead vocals, guitar, and keyboards.
2. **Barry Fratelli**: Bass guitar.
3. **Mince Fratelli**: Drums, percussion, and backing vocals.

They gained significant popularity with their debut album *Costello Music* in 2006, which included hits like "Chelsea Dagger" and "Whistle for the Choir."
