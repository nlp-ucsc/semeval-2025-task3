Lamb's lettuce, also known as **corn salad** or **Valerianella locusta**, belongs to the plant family **Caprifoliaceae** (which is also known as the honeysuckle family). This family includes various flowering plants, shrubs, and vines, and lamb's lettuce is one of the edible species within this group. It is often used in salads and is appreciated for its mild, slightly nutty flavor.
