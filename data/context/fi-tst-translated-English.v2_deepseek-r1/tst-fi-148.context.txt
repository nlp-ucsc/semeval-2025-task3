The movie "The Thin Red Line" is set during the Battle of Mount Austen, the Galvanic Operation, which took place during the Guadalcanal Campaign in World War II. The specific battle depicted in the film is the Battle of Mount Austen, part of the larger campaign where U.S. forces fought against the Imperial Japanese Army. The 1998 film, directed by Terrence Malick, portrays the intense combat and psychological struggles faced by the soldiers during this pivotal battle.
