Folorunsho Alakija is a Nigerian businesswoman who is involved in various sectors such as oil and gas, fashion, real estate, and philanthropy. She is one of the richest women in Africa and has been recognized for her significant contributions to business and entrepreneurship. Alakija is also known for her philanthropic efforts, particularly in the areas of education, healthcare, and women's empowerment through her charitable foundation. She serves as a prominent example of successful female leadership in Africa's business landscape.
