Peter Iwers, the bassist for the Swedish metal band In Flames, has mentioned several musical influences. He has cited bands such as Iron Maiden, Judas Priest, Black Sabbath, and Metallica as key influences. These bands represent the heavy metal and hard rock genres, which have had a significant impact on his playing style and musical approach. Additionally, Iwers has expressed appreciation for a variety of other artists and bands, reflecting a broad range of musical tastes that contribute to his work with In Flames.
