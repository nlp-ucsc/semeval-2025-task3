As of 2016, the Norwegian Baptist Union (Det Norske Baptistsamfunn) had approximately 97 congregations. This number reflects the union's structure and reach within Norway during that year. The Norwegian Baptist Union has been a significant presence in the country's religious landscape, promoting Baptist principles and practices. For the most accurate and up-to-date information, referring to the latest reports or official sources from Det Norske Baptistsamfunn would be recommended.
