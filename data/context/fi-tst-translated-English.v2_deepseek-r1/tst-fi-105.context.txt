VinFast, the Vietnamese automotive manufacturer, introduced its first car models at the 2018 Paris Motor Show. Specifically, the company showcased its first two models, the VinFast LUX A2.0 sedan and the VinFast LUX SA2.0 SUV. Both vehicles were well-received for their design and the fact that they were Vietnam's first domestically produced cars. This marked a significant milestone for Vietnam's automotive industry as it entered the global market.
