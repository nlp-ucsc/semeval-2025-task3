Andrei Kravtšuk is an Estonian politician who served as the Mayor of Narva from 2013 to 2015. He is a member of the Estonian Reform Party, which is one of the major political parties in Estonia. During his tenure as Mayor, Kravtšuk focused on various urban development projects, including improvements in infrastructure, public services, and local economy. His leadership was notable for its emphasis on modernizing Narva, a city with a significant Russian-speaking population, and addressing the challenges unique to the region.
