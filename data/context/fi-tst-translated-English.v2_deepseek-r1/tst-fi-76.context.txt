Fredrikstad FK, a Norwegian football club, plays its home games at Fredrikstad Stadion. The stadium is located in Fredrikstad, Norway, and has a seating capacity of approximately 12,500 spectators. It has been the home ground for the club since 1920 and has undergone several renovations to modernize its facilities. Fredrikstad Stadion is known for its traditional football atmosphere and is a significant venue in Norwegian football history.
