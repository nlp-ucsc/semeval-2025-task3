The Leftovers is a drama television series that premiere in 2014. It was created by *Damon Lindelof* and *Tom Perrotta*. The show is based on Perrotta's 2011 novel of the same name.

*The series is known for its complex storytelling, deep themes, and exploration of grief, loss, and personal identity.* It has been praised for its performances, writing, and the way it deals with ambiguous and existential questions.

The show ran for three seasons until its conclusion in 2017.
