The asteroid 5412 Rou was discovered on September 7, 1973, by Soviet astronomer Nikolai Stepanovich Chernykh. Nikolai Stepanovich Chernykh was a renowned astronomer who discovered numerous asteroids and comets during his career. The asteroid 5412 Rou is named after his wife, Lyudmila Ivanovna Rou, who was also an astronomer. This asteroid is a member of the Flora family, a group of S-type asteroids in the inner asteroid belt.
