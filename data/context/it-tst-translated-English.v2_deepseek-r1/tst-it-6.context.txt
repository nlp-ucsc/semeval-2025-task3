The movie "Tuck Everlasting" is adapted from the book of the same name, written by Natalie Babbitt. The novel was first published in 1975 and has since become a classic of children's literature. The story revolves around a young girl named Winnie Foster who discovers a family, the Tucks, that has discovered the secret to eternal life by drinking from a magical spring. The book explores themes of immortality, family, and the cycle of life. There have been two film adaptations: one in 1981 and another in 2002.
