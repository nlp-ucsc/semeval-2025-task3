The genus *Foudia* comprises several species of birds commonly known as fodies. These are small, seed-eating birds endemic to the Mascarene Islands and other Indian Ocean islands. Here are the recognized species within the *Foudia* genus:

1. **Foudia madagascariensis** - Madagascar Fody
2. **Foudia mauritiana** - Mauritius Fody
3. **Foudia rubra** - Red Fody
4. **Foudia sechellarum** - Seychelles Fody
5. **Foudia omissa** - Rodrigues Fody

Each species is typically restricted to specific islands and exhibits distinct plumage and coloration, especially during the breeding season.
