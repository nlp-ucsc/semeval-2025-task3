The nominees for the BAFTA Award for Best Supporting Actor in 1989 included:

- Michael Keaton for his role in "Beetlejuice"
- Bill Nighy for "The Occasional Man"
- Sean Connery for "Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade"
- Julian Glover for "Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade"
- Robert Duvall for "Lonesome Dove"

The winner of this category was Michael Keaton. His performance in "Beetlejuice" was particularly praised for its originality and energy.
