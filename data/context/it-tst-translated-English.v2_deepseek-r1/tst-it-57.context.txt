The city of Gödöllő is located in Pest county, Hungary. It is situated approximately 30 kilometers northeast of Budapest, the capital city of Hungary. Gödöllő is one of the largest cities in Pest county and is known for its historical significance, including the Royal Palace of Gödöllő, which was a favorite residence of Emperor Franz Joseph and Queen Elisabeth (Sisi) of Austria-Hungary. The city is also home to a railway station that serves as an important transportation hub in the region.
