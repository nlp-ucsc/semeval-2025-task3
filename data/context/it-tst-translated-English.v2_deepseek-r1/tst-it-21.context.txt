Napier, a renowned British engineering company, produced several notable H-type engines, primarily during the mid-20th century. The H-type engines were part of their lineup, known for their power and efficiency. Some of the key H engines produced by Napier include:

1. **Napier H Engine**: This was a prominent model, known for its high performance and reliability. It was widely used in various industrial and marine applications.

2. **Napier H-50 Engine**: The H-50 was another variant, offering a balance between power and fuel efficiency. It was often employed in both stationary and mobile machinery.

3. **Napier H-100 Engine**: The H-100 represented a more powerful version, designed for heavy-duty applications where higher output was required.

These engines were celebrated for their durability and versatility, contributing significantly to Napier's reputation in the engineering sector.
