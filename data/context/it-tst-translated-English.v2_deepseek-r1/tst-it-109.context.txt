HMY Britannia served as the Royal Yacht of the British monarch from 1953 to 1997, which is a total of 44 years. She was launched on April 16, 1953, and decommissioned on December 11, 1997. During her service, she played a significant role in British history, serving Queen Elizabeth II and other members of the royal family. Britannia is now moored in Edinburgh, Scotland, where she serves as a museum and a popular tourist attraction.
