The original lineup of the musical group HEALTH included:

- Jake Duzsik
- John Famiglietti
- Jupiter Keyes (lived 1986, member from 2006 to 2015)
- Benjamin Jared Miller

This lineup was active from the band's formation in 2006 up until Keyes' departure in 2015. Since then, the band has continued with various configurations, but the core members have remained Jake Duzsik, John Famiglietti, and Benjamin Jared Miller.
