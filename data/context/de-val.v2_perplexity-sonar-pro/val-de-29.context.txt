Chance the Rapper's album "Coloring Book" won the Grammy Award for Best Rap Album at the 59th Annual Grammy Awards in 2017[2][4][8]. This was a significant achievement for Chance the Rapper, as it was his first Grammy win. The album was praised for its innovative approach to hip-hop and its blend of gospel and rap elements. In addition to winning Best Rap Album, Chance the Rapper also won the award for Best New Artist at the same ceremony, further cementing his breakout success with "Coloring Book"[4][8].
